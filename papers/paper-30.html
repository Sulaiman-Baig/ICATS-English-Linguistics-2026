Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Thank You Project

Ms. Fatima gave her class a special assignment. "Write a thank you letter to someone who has helped you," she said.

Some students wrote to their parents. Others wrote to teachers or coaches. Zain decided to write to the school janitor, Mr. Riaz.

"You always keep our school clean and smile at us every morning," Zain wrote. "Thank you for making our school a happy place."

When Mr. Riaz received the letter, he had tears in his eyes. "In twenty years, this is the first thank you letter I have ever received," he said.

The class learned that kindness matters, especially to those who often go unnoticed.

Q1. What assignment did Ms. Fatima give?

Q2. Who did Zain write his letter to?

Q3. Why did Zain thank Mr. Riaz?

Q4. How did Mr. Riaz react to the letter?

Q5. What did the class learn from this project?

My Schoolbag

My schoolbag is heavy, full of things,
Books and pencils, rulers and strings.
I carry it on my back each day,
As I walk to school and play.

Inside are my favorite books,
Notebooks filled with all my looks.
Erasers, crayons, scissors too,
Everything I need to get me through!

Q6. How does the speaker describe the schoolbag?

Q7. What are some things inside the schoolbag?
