Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Balloon Adventure

Little Sana held tightly to her red balloon at the fair. It was the prettiest balloon she had ever seen. But then, a gust of wind blew it right out of her hand!

"My balloon!" Sana cried as she watched it float higher and higher into the sky.

Her father hugged her. "I know it is sad, but think about where your balloon might travel. Maybe it will float over mountains and oceans!"

Sana wiped her tears and smiled a little. "Maybe another child will find it and be happy."

That night, Sana dreamed of her red balloon having amazing adventures all around the world.

Q1Where was Sana when she lost her balloon?
Q2How did Sana lose her balloon?
Q3How did Sana feel at first?
Q4What did her father say to make her feel better?
Q5What did Sana dream about?

Poetry

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

The Rainbow

After the rain has gone away,
A rainbow comes out to play.
Red, orange, yellow, green, and blue,
With purple and violet painting the view.

An arch of colors in the sky,
A beautiful sight for you and I.
It does not last very long,
But its beauty makes us strong.

Q6When does the rainbow appear?
Q7How many colors are mentioned in the poem?
Q8Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"The colors shine so very bright,
Making everything feel just ___________."
