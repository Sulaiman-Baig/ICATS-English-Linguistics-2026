Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Helpful Robot

In the year 2050, a small robot named Beep lived with the Ahmed family. Beep was special because he loved to help. Every morning, he made breakfast. Every evening, he helped the children with homework.

One day, Beep noticed that Grandma was sad. She missed her garden, but her old legs couldn't let her work outside anymore.

"I have an idea!" said Beep. He built a small indoor garden with special lights. He planted all of Grandma's favorite flowers.

When Grandma saw the garden, tears of joy rolled down her cheeks. "Thank you, Beep," she said. "You're not just helpful, you're thoughtful."

Q1. When does this story take place?

Q2. Why was Grandma sad?

Q3. How did Beep solve Grandma's problem?

Q4. Why did Grandma say Beep was "thoughtful"?

Q5. What message does this story teach us?

Seasons Change

In spring the flowers start to bloom,
The birds return and end winter's gloom.
Summer brings the blazing sun,
Time for swimming and outdoor fun.

Autumn leaves fall red and gold,
The air gets crisp and slightly cold.
Winter wraps the world in white,
Snowflakes falling, what a sight!

Q7. What happens in autumn according to the poem?
