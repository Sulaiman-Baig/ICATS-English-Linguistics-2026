Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Wrong Bus

It was Farhan's first day taking the bus alone. He was nervous but excited. When the bus came, he hopped on and found a seat.

After a while, Farhan noticed the streets looked unfamiliar. "Excuse me," he asked the driver, "does this bus go to Green Street?"

"Green Street? Oh no, son, this is the Blue Route. You need the Red Route."

Farhan felt his heart pound. But the kind driver helped him. She called the other bus and told them to wait. When they reached the next stop, the Red Route bus was waiting!

"Thank you so much!" Farhan said.

"No problem," smiled the driver. "Everyone makes mistakes. The important thing is asking for help."

Q1. Why was Farhan nervous at the beginning?

Q2. What mistake did Farhan make?

Q3. How did Farhan realize he was on the wrong bus?

Q4. How did the bus driver help Farhan?

Q5. What lesson did Farhan learn?

The Train

Choo, choo, here comes the train,

It carries people far and wide,
Come along and take a ride!

Past the mountains, through the plains,
Nothing stops the mighty trains.
Click-clack on the tracks so long,
Singing its own travel song.
