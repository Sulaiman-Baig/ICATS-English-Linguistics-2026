Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Brave Little Ant

There was a tiny ant named Andy who lived in a big anthill. One day, dark clouds covered the sky and it started to rain heavily. Water began to flood the anthill!

"We must move to higher ground!" Andy shouted. But the other ants were too scared to move. Andy knew he had to be brave. He led the way through the storm, helping the young ants and carrying food supplies.

After many hours, the ant colony reached a safe hill. The rain stopped and the sun came out. All the ants cheered for Andy. "You saved us!" they said. Andy smiled. He learned that even the smallest creature can do big things when they are brave.

Q1 What problem did the ant colony face?
Q2 How did the other ants feel at first?
Q3 What did Andy do to help?
Q4 What happened after the rain stopped?
Q5 What is the main message of this story?

Poetry Comprehension

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

Stars at Night

When the sun goes down to sleep,
Stars come out with secrets to keep.
Twinkling high up in the sky,
Like diamonds winking from on high.

I count them one by one by one,
Until the night is almost done.
They guide the way for those who roam,
And light the path that leads back home.

Q6 When do the stars come out?
Q7 What are the stars compared to in the poem?
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"The moon shines bright throughout the night, The stars around it are a beautiful ___________."
