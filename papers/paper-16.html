Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Snowball Fight

Winter had finally arrived! Sameer woke up to find his backyard covered in white, fluffy snow. He quickly put on his warmest clothes and ran outside.

His neighbor, Hina, was already building a snow fort. "Want to have a snowball fight?" she called out.

"You are on!" Sameer shouted back.

They spent the whole morning throwing snowballs and laughing. When they got cold, Sameer's mom called them inside for hot chocolate with marshmallows.

"This is the best winter day ever!" Sameer said, warming his hands on the mug.

Q1 What did Sameer see when he woke up?
Q2 What was Hina doing when Sameer came outside?
Q3 What did they do when they got cold?
Q4 What was in the hot chocolate?
Q5 How did Sameer feel about the day?

Poetry

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

My Pet Goldfish

In a bowl of crystal clear,
My little goldfish swims so near.
Round and round she goes all day,
In her home where she loves to play.

Golden scales that shine so bright,
She sparkles in the morning light.
I tap the glass to say hello,
She wiggles her fins and says "hello" right back, you know!

Q6 Where does the goldfish live?
Q7 What color are the fish's scales?
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"I feed her every single day,
And watch her swim and splash and ___________."
