Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Rainy Day Plan

"The field trip is cancelled," announced Ms. Naheed. "It is raining too hard."

The class groaned. They had been looking forward to visiting the zoo for weeks.

"But," Ms. Naheed continued with a smile, "we are going to have a zoo in our classroom instead!"

She divided the class into groups. Each group researched a different animal and made a presentation. They drew pictures, shared facts, and even made animal sounds!

By the end of the day, everyone was laughing and learning. "This was even better than the real zoo!" said Kamran.

Q1. Why was the field trip cancelled?

Q2. How did the students feel when they heard the news?

Q3. What did Ms. Naheed do instead of the field trip?

Q4. What did the groups do?

Q5. How did Kamran feel about the classroom activity?

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

My Family

My family is like a garden,
Each one special, each one fun.
Mom is like the sunshine bright,
Dad is strong, he holds us tight.

Sister is a pretty flower,
Brother makes me laugh each hour.
Grandma tells the best stories,
Grandpa shares the morning glories.

Q6. What is the family compared to in the poem?

Q7. Who is compared to sunshine?
