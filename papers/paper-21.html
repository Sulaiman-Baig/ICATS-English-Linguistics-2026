Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Lost Teddy Bear

Ayesha loved her teddy bear, Mr. Buttons, more than anything. She had him since she was a baby. One day, after a trip to the mall, Mr. Buttons was missing!

Ayesha searched everywhere - the car, her room, the living room. Nothing. She cried herself to sleep that night.

The next morning, the phone rang. It was the mall security. "We found a teddy bear with a name tag that says Ayesha. Is it yours?"

Ayesha and her mom rushed to the mall. There was Mr. Buttons, sitting on a chair in the security office, looking like he was waiting for her!

Q1. What was the name of Ayesha's teddy bear?

Q2. Where did Ayesha lose her teddy bear?

Q3. How did the security know the teddy bear was Ayesha's?

Q4. How did Ayesha feel when she lost Mr. Buttons?

Q5. What lesson can we learn from this story?

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

Grandma's Cookies

The smell of cookies fills the air,
Grandma's baking with such care.
Chocolate chips in every bite,
Golden brown, they are just right!

She lets me help her stir the dough,
And watch the timer as minutes go.
The best part is when they are done,
Eating them together is so much fun!

Q6. What kind of cookies is Grandma making?

Q7. What does the child help Grandma do?

Q8. Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"We sit together, Grandma and me,
Having cookies with a cup of ___________."
