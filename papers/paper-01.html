Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Lost Puppy

Maya was walking home from school when she heard a soft whimper. She looked around and found a small brown puppy hiding under a bush. The puppy was shivering and looked scared.

"Don't worry, little one," Maya said softly. She gently picked up the puppy and noticed it had a blue collar with a tag. The tag said "Buddy" and had a phone number.

Maya took the puppy home and asked her mother to call the number. Soon, a happy little boy named Ali came to pick up his puppy. "Thank you so much!" Ali said with tears of joy. "Buddy ran away this morning and I was so worried!"

Maya felt warm inside. Helping others felt wonderful.

Q1 Where did Maya find the puppy?
Q2 How did Maya know the puppy's name?
Q3 Why was the puppy shivering?
Q4 How did Ali feel when he got his puppy back?
Q5 What lesson can we learn from this story?

Poetry Comprehension

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

My Little Garden

I planted a seed in the ground so deep,
I gave it water and let it sleep.
The sun came out with a golden glow,
And soon my little plant began to grow.

First came leaves of emerald green,
The prettiest sight I've ever seen.
Then a flower bloomed so bright,
Red and yellow, what a delight!

Q6 What did the speaker plant?
Q7 What helped the plant grow?
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"The butterfly came to say hello, Dancing in the breeze so ___________."

Q13 What does "butterflies in my stomach" mean in this sentence?
"Before the singing competition, Zara had butterflies in her stomach."
Q14 Read the passage and answer the question.
"Ahmed loves to help at home. Every Saturday, he cleans his room, does his homework, and then watches TV as a reward. His parents are very proud of him."

What kind of person is Ahmed?
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Q16 Which reason fits best with the opinion and example?
