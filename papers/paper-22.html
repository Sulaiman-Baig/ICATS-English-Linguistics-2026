Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Science Fair

Sana spent weeks working on her science fair project. She wanted to find out which type of music helped plants grow better - classical, rock, or no music at all.

She planted three identical seeds in identical pots. One listened to classical music, one listened to rock music, and one had no music. Every day, she measured their growth and recorded the data.

On the day of the science fair, Sana presented her findings. The plant with classical music grew the tallest! The judges were impressed with her careful research.

"First place goes to Sana!" the announcer said. Sana beamed with pride. All her hard work had paid off.

Q2. How many plants did Sana use in her experiment?

Q3. What did Sana do every day?

Q4. Which plant grew the tallest?

Q5. What place did Sana win?

The Little Seed

I am a little seed, so small and round,
Buried in the dark, cold ground.
But do not worry, do not fear,
Spring is coming, spring is near!

The rain will water, the sun will warm,
I will be safe from any storm.
And one fine day, I will break free,
And grow into a beautiful tree!

Q7. What season is the seed waiting for?
