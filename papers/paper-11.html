Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Secret Garden Door

Emma had lived in her grandmother's old house for a year, but she never noticed the small wooden door hidden behind the rose bushes. One rainy afternoon, while playing hide and seek with her cat Whiskers, she discovered it.

The door was painted blue, though the paint was peeling. A rusty key hung from a nail nearby. Emma's heart raced as she turned the key and pushed the door open.

Behind it was the most beautiful garden she had ever seen. Butterflies danced among colorful flowers, a small fountain bubbled in the center, and fruit trees lined the walls.

"This was my secret garden when I was your age," said Grandmother, appearing behind her. "Now it belongs to you."

Q1. How did Emma discover the hidden door?

Q2. What was hanging near the door?

Q3. What did Emma find behind the door?

Q4. Who did the garden belong to before Emma?

Q5. What word best describes how Emma felt when she opened the door?

Morning Song

Wake up, wake up, come out to play!
The dewdrops sparkle on the grass,
The morning hours quickly pass.

Birds are singing in the trees,
Dancing flowers in the breeze.
A brand new day has just begun,
Full of laughter, full of fun.
