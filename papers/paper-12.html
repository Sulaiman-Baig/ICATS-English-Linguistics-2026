Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Little Chef

Seven-year-old Amir loved watching cooking shows on TV. One day, he decided to make breakfast for his family.

He woke up early and tiptoed to the kitchen. First, he got out the bread. Then, he carefully cracked eggs into a bowl (only dropping one shell in by accident). He asked his older sister to help with the stove.

Together, they made scrambled eggs and toast. Amir also poured orange juice into glasses. He arranged everything on a tray with a flower from the garden.

When Mom and Dad came to the table, they were amazed. "Did you make all this?" Mom asked.

Amir nodded proudly. "With a little help from Sis!"

Q1. What inspired Amir to cook?

Q2. What went wrong when Amir cracked the eggs?

Q3. Why did Amir need his sister's help?

Q4. What special touch did Amir add to the tray?

Q5. How did Amir feel when he served breakfast?

The Kite

Up, up, up goes my kite so high,
A colorful dot in the bright blue sky.
The wind is strong, it pulls the string,
I hold on tight to this wonderful thing.

It dips and dives, it loops and sways,
I could watch it fly for days and days.
When the wind dies down, it comes back to me,
The happiest flyer you will ever see!

Q6. What makes the kite fly high?

Q7. What does the speaker do when the wind pulls the string?
