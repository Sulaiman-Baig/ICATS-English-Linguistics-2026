Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Birthday Surprise

Maryam was turning seven years old. She woke up expecting a big celebration, but the house was quiet. "Happy birthday," Mom said calmly, giving her a small hug.

At breakfast, no one mentioned a party. At school, her friends seemed normal. Maryam felt disappointed. "Did everyone forget?" she wondered.

When she came home, the house was dark. Suddenly, the lights turned on! "SURPRISE!" everyone shouted. Her whole family and all her friends were there! Balloons, streamers, and a giant chocolate cake filled the room.

Maryam's eyes filled with happy tears. "This is the best surprise ever!"

Q1How did Maryam feel when she woke up?
Q2Why did Maryam feel disappointed during the day?
Q3What was the surprise?
Q4What kind of cake did she have?
Q5How did Maryam feel at the end?

Poetry

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

My Umbrella

Pitter, patter goes the rain,
Tapping softly on my windowpane.
I grab my umbrella, bright and blue,
And splash in puddles, one and two!

The umbrella keeps me nice and dry,
While raindrops fall down from the sky.
I love the rain, I love to play,
Every rainy day is a special day!

Q6What sound does the rain make in the poem?
Q7What color is the umbrella?
Q8Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"After the rain, the sun comes out,
And children play and run ___________."
