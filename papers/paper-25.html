Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The New Baby

Haris was excited when Mom and Dad brought home his new baby sister, Zoya. But after a few days, he started feeling different.

Everyone paid attention to Zoya. Visitors brought gifts for her, not him. Mom and Dad seemed too busy to play with Haris.

"I wish she would go back," Haris thought one night.

Then one morning, Zoya grabbed Haris's finger and smiled at him. It was her first real smile!

"She smiled at me!" Haris shouted happily.

"Of course," said Mom. "She loves her big brother."

Suddenly, Haris did not feel jealous anymore. He felt important.

Q1. What new member joined Haris's family?

Q2. Why did Haris start feeling unhappy?

Q3. What did Haris wish would happen?

Q4. What made Haris feel better?

Q5. How did Haris feel at the end?

The Butterfly

Once I was a caterpillar,
Crawling on the ground.
I munched on leaves all day long,
Making crunchy sounds.

Then I wrapped myself up tight,
In a cozy cocoon bed.
When I woke up one fine day,
Beautiful wings I spread!

Q6. What was the narrator before becoming a butterfly?

Q7. What did the caterpillar eat?
