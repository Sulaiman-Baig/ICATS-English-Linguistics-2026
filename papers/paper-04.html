Reading Comprehension

Read the following story and answer questions 1 through 5.

The Magic Seeds

Old farmer Hassan had a problem. His crops wouldn't grow no matter how hard he tried. One day, a traveler gave him three golden seeds.

"Plant these with love and patience," said the traveler. "Water them every day and sing to them."

Hassan thought this was strange, but he tried it anyway. He planted the seeds, watered them daily, and sang old songs his mother had taught him.

To his amazement, the seeds grew into the most beautiful plants anyone had ever seen. They produced fruits that were sweeter than honey. People came from far away to taste them.

"The magic wasn't in the seeds," Hassan realized. "It was in the love and care I gave them."

Q1What was farmer Hassan's problem?
Q2What did the traveler tell Hassan to do?
Q3Why did Hassan think the traveler's advice was strange?
Q4What made the plants grow so well?
Q5What can we learn from this story?

Poetry Comprehension

Read the following poem and answer questions 6 through 8.

My Shadow

I have a little shadow that goes in and out with me,
And what can be the use of him is more than I can see.
He is very, very like me from my head down to my feet,
And I see him jump before me when I jump into the street.

He stays so close beside me, he's a coward you can see,
He'd think it was a shame to stick to me the way he does!
One morning very early, before the sun was up,
I rose and found my shadow had stayed behind in bed.

Q6What is the poem about?
Q7When did the shadow disappear?
Q8Complete the poem with a word that rhymes:
"In the bright afternoon light, My shadow stretches far out of ___________."

The New Student

Everyone stared at me when I walked into the classroom. I felt my cheeks turn red. The teacher smiled and said, "Class, this is your new friend."

I found an empty desk and sat down. A girl next to me whispered, "Hi! I'm Sana. Don't worry, everyone is nice here." She shared her crayons with me during art time.

By the end of the day, I had made three new friends. Maybe this new school wouldn't be so scary after all.
