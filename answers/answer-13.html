Reading Passage: "The Lost Library Book" (Q1-5)

Zara had borrowed a book from the school library three weeks ago. Now it was due, and she could not find it anywhere! She looked under her bed, in her closet, and behind the sofa. No book.

"I will have to tell the librarian," Zara said sadly. She imagined having to pay for the lost book with all her saved pocket money.

At school, she walked slowly to the library. "Mrs. Ahmed, I am so sorry. I lost my book," she confessed.

Mrs. Ahmed smiled kindly. "Which book was it, dear?"

"The Magic Tree," Zara answered.

Mrs. Ahmed laughed gently. "Zara, you returned that book last week! You were the first one to return it on Monday. Do you not remember?"

Zara's face turned bright red, but then she laughed too. What a relief!

Q1 What was Zara worried about?
Correct Answer: B) A lost library book
Zara was worried because she could not find her library book.
Q2 Where did Zara look for the book?
Correct Answer: B) Under her bed, in her closet, and behind the sofa
She looked in all three places.
Q3 What did Zara expect to happen when she told the librarian?
Correct Answer: B) She would have to pay for the book
"She imagined having to pay for the lost book."
Q4 What was the truth about the book?
Correct Answer: B) Zara had already returned it
"You returned that book last week!"
Q5 How did Zara feel at the end of the story?
Correct Answer: C) Relieved and amused
"What a relief!" and she laughed.

Poem: "The Moon" (Q6-8)

The moon is like a silver ball,
Watching over one and all.
It glows so bright up in the night,
A lovely, peaceful, calming sight.

Sometimes it is round, sometimes it is thin,
A crescent with a sleepy grin.
But whatever shape the moon may take,
It is there for us when we are awake.

Q6 What is the moon compared to in the poem?
Correct Answer: B) A silver ball
"The moon is like a silver ball."
Q7 According to the poem, what shapes can the moon be?
Correct Answer: C) Round and thin (crescent)
"Sometimes it is round, sometimes it is thin, A crescent."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Stars twinkle in the sky at night, Shining with their silver ___________."
Correct Answer: D) light
"Light" rhymes with "night."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? The magician pulled a rabbit out of his hat. Children gasped with amazement. Colorful balloons decorated the stage of the community center.
Correct Answer: A) A magic show
Magician, rabbit from hat, amazed children = magic show.
Q10 You want to persuade your friend to join the school choir. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Singing in the choir is so much fun, and you have a beautiful voice!
Persuasive - gives benefit and compliment.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Next, I colored the picture with crayons. 2. Finally, I showed my drawing to Mom. 3. First, I drew a picture of a house. 4. Then, I added a sun and some clouds.
Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2
Drawing: First (3) drew house, Then (4) added sun/clouds, Next (1) colored, Finally (2) showed Mom.
Q12 Read the paragraph and add the best topic sentence: ___. First, gather all the ingredients you need. Then, follow the recipe step by step. Do not forget to preheat the oven if required. Finally, let your creation cool before eating.
Correct Answer: A) Baking is easy if you follow some simple steps.
The paragraph explains baking steps.
Q13 What does "costs an arm and a leg" mean in: Mom said the new bicycle costs an arm and a leg.
Correct Answer: B) It is very expensive
"Costs an arm and a leg" = very expensive.
Q14 Hamza stared at the sunny day outside his window. His friends were playing cricket in the street. But his math homework sat unfinished on the desk. He picked up his pencil and got to work. What did Hamza decide to do?
Correct Answer: B) Finish his homework first
"He picked up his pencil and got to work."
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) "May I please have some water?" asked the child.
Correct quotation marks, question mark inside, lowercase "asked."
Q16 Which reason fits best? Music makes people happy. ___. For example, when I hear my favorite song, I can not help but smile and dance.
Correct Answer: B) It can change your mood and lift your spirits.
This explains why music makes people happy.

Reading Passage: "Gym Class" (Q17-18)

I bounced down the hall, ready for my favorite part of the day. Gym class! I love running on the track and playing basketball with my friends.

Today, we learned how to do cartwheels. I wobbled at first, but by the end of class, I could do one without falling. Coach Tariq gave me a high five!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A student
The narrator goes to gym class and gets a high five from the coach.
Q18 What is the narrator's favorite part of school?
Correct Answer: C) Gym class
"My favorite part of the day. Gym class!"

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about books. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Why did the book go to the doctor? Because it had too many problems!
Wordplay on "problems" - math problems in books vs health problems.
Q20 Choose the best reply: Aunt: How was your day at school? Child: ___
Correct Answer: B) It was great! We learned about dinosaurs in science class.
A positive, engaging response with details.
