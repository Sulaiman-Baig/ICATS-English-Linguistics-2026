Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Lost Library Book" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) A lost library book

Zara was worried because she could not find her library book.

Q2Correct Answer: B) Under her bed, in her closet, and behind the sofa

She looked in all three places.

Q3Correct Answer: B) She would have to pay for the book

"She imagined having to pay for the lost book."

Q4Correct Answer: B) Zara had already returned it

"You returned that book last week!"

Q5Correct Answer: C) Relieved and amused

"What a relief!" and she laughed.

Poem: "The Moon" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) A silver ball

"The moon is like a silver ball."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Round and thin (crescent)

"Sometimes it is round, sometimes it is thin, A crescent."

Q8Correct Answer: D) light

"Light" rhymes with "night."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A magic show

Magician, rabbit from hat, amazed children = magic show.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Singing in the choir is so much fun, and you have a beautiful voice!

Persuasive - gives benefit and compliment.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2

Drawing: First (3) drew house, Then (4) added sun/clouds, Next (1) colored, Finally (2) showed Mom.

Q12Correct Answer: A) Baking is easy if you follow some simple steps.

The paragraph explains baking steps.

Q13Correct Answer: B) It is very expensive

"Costs an arm and a leg" = very expensive.

Q14Correct Answer: B) Finish his homework first

"He picked up his pencil and got to work."

Q15Correct Answer: C) "May I please have some water?" asked the child.

Correct quotation marks, question mark inside, lowercase "asked."

Q16Correct Answer: B) It can change your mood and lift your spirits.

This explains why music makes people happy.

Reading Passage: "Gym Class" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A student

The narrator goes to gym class and gets a high five from the coach.

Q18Correct Answer: C) Gym class

"My favorite part of the day. Gym class!"

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the book go to the doctor? Because it had too many problems!

Wordplay on "problems" - math problems in books vs health problems.

Q20Correct Answer: B) It was great! We learned about dinosaurs in science class.

A positive, engaging response with details.
