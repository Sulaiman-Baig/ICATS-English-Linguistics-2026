Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Helpful Neighbor" (Q1-5)

Mrs. Khatoon was an elderly woman who lived alone next door. One winter morning, Bilal noticed that snow had piled up on her driveway.

"Mom, can I shovel Mrs. Khatoon's driveway?" Bilal asked.

"That is very thoughtful of you," Mom smiled.

Bilal worked hard for an hour, clearing all the snow. When he finished, Mrs. Khatoon opened her door with a big smile.

"Thank you so much, Bilal! Please come in for some warm soup and cookies."

As Bilal sat in her cozy kitchen, Mrs. Khatoon told him stories about when she was young. Bilal realized that spending time with her was even better than the cookies!

Q1 What problem did Mrs. Khatoon have?
Correct Answer: B) Snow had piled up on her driveway
"One winter morning, Bilal noticed that snow had piled up on her driveway."
Q2 What did Bilal offer to do?
Correct Answer: B) Shovel her driveway
"Can I shovel Mrs. Khatoon's driveway?"
Q3 How long did Bilal work to clear the snow?
Correct Answer: B) An hour
"Bilal worked hard for an hour."
Q4 What did Mrs. Khatoon give Bilal?
Correct Answer: C) Soup and cookies, and her company
"Please come in for some warm soup and cookies." Plus he enjoyed her stories.
Q5 What did Bilal enjoy most?
Correct Answer: C) Spending time with Mrs. Khatoon and her stories
"Bilal realized that spending time with her was even better than the cookies!"

Poem: "Spring Has Come" (Q6-8)

Flowers blooming everywhere,
Sweet perfume fills the air.
Baby birds begin to sing,
Welcome, welcome, lovely spring!

Butterflies with painted wings,
Dancing over everything.
Trees are dressed in shades of green,
The prettiest sight I have ever seen.

Q6 What season is this poem about?
Correct Answer: C) Spring
"Welcome, welcome, lovely spring!"
Q7 What are the butterflies doing in the poem?
Correct Answer: B) Dancing
"Butterflies with painted wings, Dancing over everything."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Bees are buzzing all around, Making such a happy ___________."
Correct Answer: B) sound
"Sound" rhymes with "around."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "Actors in colorful costumes stood on the stage. The curtain rose as the audience clapped. Bright lights illuminated the performers."
Correct Answer: B) A stage theater
Actors, stage, curtain, audience = theater.
Q10 You want to persuade your teacher to give less homework. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) With less homework, we could have more time to review what we learned in class and rest properly.
Persuasive with logical reasons.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, I brushed his fur until it was shiny. 2. Finally, I gave him a treat for being so good. 3. First, I filled the tub with warm water. 4. Next, I washed my dog with special shampoo.
Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2
Dog bath: fill tub (3), wash (4), brush fur (1), give treat (2).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. They give us light when the sun goes down. Without electricity, many of our devices would not work. We should remember to turn off lights when we leave a room to save electricity."
Correct Answer: B) Lights are important in our daily lives.
Topic sentence about lights matches the paragraph.
Q13 What does "on cloud nine" mean? "After winning the spelling bee, Ahmad was on cloud nine."
Correct Answer: B) Ahmad was extremely happy
"On cloud nine" = extremely happy.
Q14 What does this passage teach us? "Fatima studied every night for two weeks. She made flashcards, practiced problems, and asked her teacher questions. When the results came, she had the highest score in the class."
Correct Answer: B) Hard work leads to success
Fatima studied hard and got the highest score.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) We have three pets: a dog, a cat, and a bird.
Correct use of colon and commas in a list.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Learning to cook is a valuable skill. _______. For example, I can now make my own lunch instead of buying expensive food."
Correct Answer: B) It helps you become more independent and save money.
Connects cooking skill to independence and saving money.

Reading Passage: "The Ocean Floor" (Q17-18)

It is dark down here at the bottom of the ocean, but my body makes its own light! I glow in bright blue and green, like a living lantern. It helps me find food and communicate with others like me.

A big fish swam by, but I scared it away with my glowing light. Life in the deep sea is full of surprises!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A deep-sea fish or creature that glows
Lives at bottom of ocean, makes own light = bioluminescent creature.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator lives at the bottom of the ocean and makes its own light
These are characteristics of deep-sea creatures.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about teeth. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What does the tooth fairy give sharks? Sand dollars!
A pun on "sand dollars" (sea creatures that look like coins).
Q20 Choose the best reply: Doctor: "Open your mouth and say 'ahh.'" Patient: ___
Correct Answer: B) Ahh.
Cooperative response following the doctor's instruction.
