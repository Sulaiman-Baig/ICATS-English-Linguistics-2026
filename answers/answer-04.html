Reading Passage: "The Magic Seeds" (Q1-5)

Old farmer Hassan had a problem. His crops wouldn't grow no matter how hard he tried. One day, a traveler gave him three golden seeds.

"Plant these with love and patience," said the traveler. "Water them every day and sing to them."

Hassan thought this was strange, but he tried it anyway. He planted the seeds, watered them daily, and sang old songs his mother had taught him.

To his amazement, the seeds grew into the most beautiful plants anyone had ever seen. They produced fruits that were sweeter than honey. People came from far away to taste them.

"The magic wasn't in the seeds," Hassan realized. "It was in the love and care I gave them."

Q1

What was farmer Hassan's problem?

AHe had no seeds
BHis crops wouldn't grow
CHe was too old to farm
DThere was no water
✓ B) His crops wouldn't grow
Explanation

"His crops wouldn't grow no matter how hard he tried" - this was Hassan's main problem.

Why other options are wrong:
A)He had seeds - they just wouldn't grow.
C)His age is not mentioned as a problem.
D)Water shortage is not mentioned.
Q2

What did the traveler tell Hassan to do?

ABuy new land
BGive up farming
CPlant with love, water daily, and sing
DSell the seeds
✓ C) Plant with love, water daily, and sing
Explanation

"Plant these with love and patience. Water them every day and sing to them."

Why other options are wrong:
A)Buying land is not mentioned.
B)The traveler encouraged farming, not quitting.
D)He gave seeds, not told to sell them.
Q3

Why did Hassan think the advice was strange?

ABecause singing to plants seemed unusual
BBecause the seeds were golden
CBecause the traveler was old
DBecause it was raining
✓ A) Because singing to plants seemed unusual
Explanation

"Hassan thought this was strange" - referring to the advice to sing to plants.

Why other options are wrong:
B)The golden color wasn't why it was strange.
C)Traveler's age is not mentioned.
D)Rain is not mentioned in the story.
Q4

What made the plants grow so well?

AMagic from the seeds
BHassan's love, care, and patience
CThe rain
DHelp from neighbors
✓ B) Hassan's love, care, and patience
Explanation

"The magic wasn't in the seeds. It was in the love and care I gave them."

Why other options are wrong:
A)Hassan realized it wasn't magic seeds.
C)Rain is not mentioned.
D)Neighbors helping is not mentioned.
Q5

What can we learn from this story?

AMagic seeds are easy to find
BSinging is useless
CLove and care can make wonderful things happen
DFarmers should not listen to travelers
✓ C) Love and care can make wonderful things happen
Explanation

The moral of the story: love and care (not magic) made beautiful things grow.

Why other options are wrong:
A)The story says magic wasn't in the seeds.
B)Singing helped - opposite message.
D)Hassan benefited from listening.

Poem Section

Poem: "My Shadow" (Q6-8)

I have a little shadow that goes in and out with me,
And what can be the use of him is more than I can see.
He is very, very like me from my head down to my feet,
And I see him jump before me when I jump into the street.

He stays so close beside me, he's a coward you can see,
He'd think it was a shame to stick to me the way he does!
One morning very early, before the sun was up,
I rose and found my shadow had stayed behind in bed.

Q6

What is the poem about?

AA friend who follows the speaker
BThe speaker's shadow
CA pet dog
DA brother
✓ B) The speaker's shadow
Explanation

"I have a little shadow" - the poem is about the speaker's shadow.

Why other options are wrong:
A)It's about a shadow, not a human friend.
C)A pet dog is not mentioned.
D)A brother is not mentioned.
Q7

When did the shadow disappear?

AAt night
BEarly morning before sunrise
CAt noon
DDuring dinner
✓ B) Early morning before sunrise
Explanation

"One morning very early, before the sun was up" - no sun means no shadow!

Why other options are wrong:
A)Poem specifically mentions morning.
C)At noon the sun is brightest - shadows exist.
D)Dinner time is not mentioned.
Q8

Complete with a word that rhymes: "In the bright afternoon light, My shadow stretches far out of ___________."

Aview
Bsight
Cday
Dway
✓ B) sight
Explanation

"Sight" rhymes with "light" - both end with the "-ight" sound.

Why other options are wrong:
A)"View" doesn't rhyme with "light".
C)"Day" doesn't rhyme with "light".
D)"Way" doesn't rhyme with "light".

Q9

Which setting matches: helmets, knee pads, skateboards, ramps?

AA swimming pool
BA skate park
CA library
DA hospital
✓ B) A skate park
Explanation

Helmets, knee pads, skateboards, ramps and rails = all skate park items!

Why other options are wrong:
A)Swimming pools don't have skateboards.
C)Libraries are quiet, no skateboarding.
D)Hospitals are for medical care.
Q10

Persuade a friend to try a new fruit. What should you write?

AFruits grow on trees.
BThis fruit is so sweet and juicy - you'll love it!
CFruits have seeds inside.
DSome fruits are red.
✓ B) This fruit is so sweet and juicy - you'll love it!
Explanation

Persuasion uses benefits and enthusiasm to convince someone.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Just a fact - not persuasive.
C)Just a fact - not persuasive.
D)Just a fact - not persuasive.
Q11

Order: 1. Next, mix ingredients. 2. Finally, decorate. 3. First, gather flour, eggs, sugar. 4. Then, bake in oven.

A3, 1, 4, 2
B1, 2, 3, 4
C3, 4, 1, 2
D2, 3, 4, 1
✓ A) 3, 1, 4, 2
Explanation

First (3), Next (1), Then (4), Finally (2) - logical baking order.

Why other options are wrong:
B)"First" (3) should come first.
C)Must mix before baking.
D)Can't decorate first.
Q12

Best topic sentence for dental care paragraph?

ATeeth are white.
BTaking care of your teeth is very important.
CDentists work in clinics.
DSome people don't like going to the dentist.
✓ B) Taking care of your teeth is very important.
Explanation

This introduces the main idea - dental hygiene tips that follow.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Too narrow - doesn't introduce care tips.
C)About dentists, not about caring for teeth.
D)Negative tone - doesn't match content.
Q13

What does "under the weather" mean?

AStanding in the rain
BOmar is not feeling well
COmar likes rainy days
DWatching weather on TV
✓ B) Omar is not feeling well
Explanation

"Under the weather" is an idiom meaning feeling sick or unwell.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Literal interpretation - not the idiom meaning.
C)Not about liking rain.
D)Not about TV weather.
Q14

Why did mother turn on the nightlight for Sam?

AShe liked bright rooms
BTo help Sam feel safe
CSam asked for a story
DTo find her book
✓ B) To help Sam feel safe
Explanation

Sam was afraid of the dark, and the nightlight "made Sam feel safe and brave."

Why other options are wrong:
A)It was for Sam, not mother's preference.
C)Story was separate from the nightlight reason.
D)Not mentioned as a reason.
Q15

Choose the sentence with correct punctuation.

Ai can't wait for summer vacation
BI cant wait for summer vacation.
CI can't wait for summer vacation!
Di Can't Wait For Summer vacation!
✓ C) I can't wait for summer vacation!
Explanation

Correct: capital I, apostrophe in "can't", exclamation mark for excitement.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Missing capital I and end punctuation.
B)Missing apostrophe in "can't".
D)Wrong capitalization throughout.
Q16

Which reason fits: "Swimming is great exercise. ___. For example, it makes arms and legs stronger."

ASwimming pools have water.
BIt helps keep your body healthy and strong.
CMany people are afraid of water.
DSwimming requires a swimsuit.
✓ B) It helps keep your body healthy and strong.
Explanation

The example about stronger arms/legs supports the reason about being healthy and strong.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Irrelevant to exercise benefits.
C)Negative - doesn't support the topic.
D)Irrelevant to exercise benefits.

Reading Comprehension - Part 2

Reading: "The New Student" (Q17-18)

Everyone stared at me when I walked into the classroom. I felt my cheeks turn red. The teacher smiled and said, "Class, this is your new friend."

I found an empty desk and sat down. A girl next to me whispered, "Hi! I'm Sana. Don't worry, everyone is nice here." She shared her crayons with me during art time.

By the end of the day, I had made three new friends. Maybe this new school wouldn't be so scary after all.

Q17

Who is telling this story?

AThe teacher
BSana
CA new student
DThe principal
✓ C) A new student
Explanation

"Everyone stared at me when I walked into the classroom" - the narrator is the new student.

Why other options are wrong:
A)Teacher spoke to the narrator.
B)Sana spoke to the narrator.
D)Principal is not mentioned.
Q18

How did the narrator feel at beginning vs end?

AHappy at first, then sad
BNervous at first, then happy
CAngry all day
DSleepy all day
✓ B) Nervous at first, then happy
Explanation

Cheeks turned red (nervous) made three friends and "wouldn't be so scary" (happy).

Why other options are wrong:
A)Opposite - started nervous, ended happy.
C)Anger is not mentioned.
D)Sleepiness is not mentioned.

✓ B) Why did the cow jump over the moon? Because the farmer had cold hands!

This is a joke with a punchline - meant to entertain and make people laugh!

Why other options are wrong:

D)This is a fact, not humor.

AI don't like this store.
BYes, please. I'm looking for a red notebook.
CStores are boring.
DI was just walking around.
✓ B) Yes, please. I'm looking for a red notebook.
