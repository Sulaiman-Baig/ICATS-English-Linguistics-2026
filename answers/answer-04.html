Reading: "The Magic Seeds"

Q1What was farmer Hassan's problem?
A) He had no seeds - Wrong: He had seeds but they wouldn't grow
B) His crops wouldn't grow - CORRECT: "His crops wouldn't grow no matter how hard he tried"
C) He was too old to farm - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) There was no water - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q2What did the traveler tell Hassan to do?
A) Buy new land - Wrong: Not mentioned
B) Give up farming - Wrong: Opposite advice
C) Plant with love, water daily, and sing - CORRECT: "Plant these with love and patience, Water them every day and sing to them"
D) Sell the seeds - Wrong: He gave seeds, didn't suggest selling
Q3Why did Hassan think the advice was strange?
A) Because singing to plants seemed unusual - CORRECT: "Hassan thought this was strange"
B) Because the seeds were golden - Wrong: Not why it was strange
C) Because the traveler was old - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) Because it was raining - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q4What made the plants grow so well?
A) Magic from the seeds - Wrong: Hassan realized it wasn't the seeds
B) Hassan's love, care, and patience - CORRECT: "The magic wasn't in the seeds... It was in the love and care I gave them"
C) The rain - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) Help from neighbors - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q5What can we learn from this story?
A) Magic seeds are easy to find - Wrong: Not the lesson
B) Singing is useless - Wrong: Opposite message
C) Love and care can make wonderful things happen - CORRECT: The moral of the story
D) Farmers should not listen to travelers - Wrong: Hassan benefited from listening

Poem: "My Shadow"

Q6What is the poem about?
A) A friend who follows the speaker - Wrong: It's about a shadow
B) The speaker's shadow - CORRECT: "I have a little shadow"
C) A pet dog - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) A brother - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q7When did the shadow disappear?
A) At night - Wrong: Specific time mentioned
B) Early morning before sunrise - CORRECT: "One morning very early, before the sun was up"
C) At noon - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) During dinner - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q8Rhyme for "light"
A) view - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme
B) sight - CORRECT: Rhymes with "light"
C) day - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme
D) way - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme with "light"

Language Skills

Q9Setting: helmets, knee pads, skateboards, ramps
A) A swimming pool - Wrong: No skateboarding
B) A skate park - CORRECT: Helmets, knee pads, skateboards, ramps and rails
C) A library - Wrong: No skateboarding
D) A hospital - Wrong: Not a recreational area
Q10Persuade friend to try new fruit
A) Fruits grow on trees - Wrong: Just a fact
B) This fruit is so sweet and juicy - you'll love it! - CORRECT: Persuasive with benefits
C) Fruits have seeds inside - Wrong: Just a fact
D) Some fruits are red - Wrong: Just a fact
Q11Order sentences: baking a cake
A) 3, 1, 4, 2 - CORRECT: First gather (3), Next mix (1), Then bake (4), Finally decorate (2)
B) 1, 2, 3, 4 - Wrong: Doesn't follow sequence words
C) 3, 4, 1, 2 - Wrong: Must mix before baking
D) 2, 3, 4, 1 - Wrong: Can't decorate first
Q12Topic sentence about dental care
A) Teeth are white - Wrong: Too narrow
B) Taking care of your teeth is very important - CORRECT: Introduces dental hygiene tips
C) Dentists work in clinics - Wrong: Not about care
D) Some people don't like going to the dentist - Wrong: Negative tone
Q13"Under the weather" meaning
A) Standing in the rain - Wrong: Literal interpretation
B) Omar is not feeling well - CORRECT: This idiom means feeling sick
C) Omar likes rainy days - Wrong: Not the meaning
D) Watching weather on TV - Wrong: Not the meaning
Q14Why did mother turn on nightlight?
A) She liked bright rooms - Wrong: Not the reason
B) To help Sam feel safe - CORRECT: Sam was afraid of dark, light made him feel safe
C) Sam asked for a story - Wrong: Story was separate
D) To find her book - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q15Correct punctuation
A) i can't wait for summer vacation - Wrong: No capital, no punctuation
B) I cant wait for summer vacation. - Wrong: Missing apostrophe
C) I can't wait for summer vacation! - CORRECT: Capital, apostrophe, exclamation
D) i Can't Wait For Summer vacation! - Wrong: Wrong capitalization
Q16Reason for "swimming is great exercise"
A) Swimming pools have water - Wrong: Irrelevant
B) It helps keep your body healthy and strong - CORRECT: Matches example about arms/legs
C) Many people are afraid of water - Wrong: Negative
D) Swimming requires a swimsuit - Wrong: Irrelevant

Reading: "The New Student"

Q17Who is telling this story?
A) The teacher - Wrong: Teacher spoke to the narrator
B) Sana - Wrong: Sana spoke to the narrator
C) A new student - CORRECT: "Everyone stared at me when I walked into the classroom"
D) The principal - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q18Narrator's feelings: beginning vs end
A) Happy at first, then sad - Wrong: Opposite
B) Nervous at first, then happy - CORRECT: Cheeks turned red (nervous), made three friends (happy)
C) Angry all day - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) Sleepy all day - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q19Entertain with moon joke
A) The moon orbits around the Earth - Wrong: Information
B) Why did the cow jump over the moon? Because the farmer had cold hands! - CORRECT: A joke
C) The moon has no light of its own - Wrong: Information
D) Astronauts have walked on the moon - Wrong: Information
Q20Reply to "Can I help you find something?"
A) I don't like this store - Wrong: Rude
B) Yes, please. I'm looking for a red notebook. - CORRECT: Polite, specific request
C) Stores are boring - Wrong: Rude
D) I was just walking around - Wrong: Dismissive
