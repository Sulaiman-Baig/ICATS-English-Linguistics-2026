Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Little Chef" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) Watching cooking shows on TV

"Amir loved watching cooking shows on TV."

Q2Correct Answer: B) Some shell fell into the bowl

"Only dropping one shell in by accident."

Q3Correct Answer: C) To use the stove safely

"He asked his older sister to help with the stove."

Q4Correct Answer: C) A flower from the garden

"He arranged everything on a tray with a flower from the garden."

Q5Correct Answer: C) Proud

"Amir nodded proudly."

Poem: "The Kite" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) The wind

"The wind is strong, it pulls the string."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Holds on tight

"I hold on tight to this wonderful thing."

Q8Correct Answer: C) sight

"Sight" rhymes with "light."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: C) A zoo

Animals in cages, monkeys, zookeeper feeding penguins = zoo.

Q10Correct Answer: B) A sleepover would be so much fun, and I promise we will be quiet...

Persuasive - gives benefits and addresses parents' concerns.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 3, 1, 4, 2

Bedtime: First (3) bath, Then (1) pajamas, Next (4) brush teeth, Finally (2) sleep.

Q12Correct Answer: A) Turtles have special bodies that keep them safe.

Topic sentence about turtle protection matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Stay positive and do not be sad

"Keep your chin up" means remain hopeful and positive.

Q14Correct Answer: B) A tired child falling asleep after playing

Main idea: child played all day, got tired, fell asleep.

Q15Correct Answer: B) I have two sisters named Aisha and Maria.

Proper nouns (names) capitalized, correct punctuation.

Q16Correct Answer: B) You have to take care of another living creature.

Explains why pets teach responsibility.

Reading Passage: "A Quiet Afternoon" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: C) A cat

Whiskers, furry body, napping, chasing red dot (laser pointer) = cat.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator has whiskers, a furry body, naps, and chases a red dot

All these are cat characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why do candles go on top of birthday cakes? Because it is hard to put them on the bottom!

A funny, logical joke about birthday cakes.

Q20Correct Answer: B) Hi! I am [name]. Welcome to our class! Want me to show you around?

Friendly, welcoming response to a new classmate.
