Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Science Fair" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) How music affects plant growth

Sana tested classical, rock, and no music on plants.

Q2Correct Answer: C) Three

"She planted three identical seeds in identical pots."

Q3Correct Answer: B) Measured the plants and recorded data

"Every day, she measured their growth and recorded the data."

Q4Correct Answer: B) The one with classical music

"The plant with classical music grew the tallest!"

Q5Correct Answer: C) First place

"First place goes to Sana!"

Poem: "The Little Seed" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: C) Buried in the ground

"Buried in the dark, cold ground."

Q7Correct Answer: D) Spring

"Spring is coming, spring is near!"

Q8Correct Answer: B) all

"All" rhymes with "tall."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A doctor's office

Nurse, temperature, health posters, doctor = medical setting.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Can we please go to the zoo?...

Persuasive with reasons (see elephants, learn about animals).

Q11Correct Answer: A) 3, 1, 4, 2

Halloween: decided costume (3), put on costume (1), got bucket (4), trick-or-treating (2).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Kites are fun toys that fly in the sky.

Topic sentence about kites matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Make people feel more comfortable

"Break the ice" = ease social tension.

Q14Correct Answer: B) The movie was touching and emotional

"The story...had touched everyone's heart."

Q15Correct Answer: C) Look at that beautiful sunset!

Exclamatory sentence needs exclamation mark.

Q16Correct Answer: B) They teach cooperation and teamwork.

Connects to example of working with others in soccer.

Reading Passage: "Building the Web" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A spider

Spins silk, makes web, catches flies = spider.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator spins silk, makes a web, and catches flies

Classic spider characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the sun go to school? To get a little brighter!

A pun on "brighter" (smarter and more luminous).

Q20Correct Answer: B) Thank you so much, Grandma! You are the best!

Grateful and loving response.
