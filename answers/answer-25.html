Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The New Baby" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) A baby sister

"Mom and Dad brought home his new baby sister, Zoya."

Q2Correct Answer: B) Everyone paid attention to the new baby

"Everyone paid attention to Zoya. Visitors brought gifts for her, not him."

Q3Correct Answer: B) The baby would go back

"I wish she would go back," Haris thought.

Q4Correct Answer: B) The baby smiled at him for the first time

"Zoya grabbed Haris's finger and smiled at him. It was her first real smile!"

Q5Correct Answer: B) Important and happy

"Haris did not feel jealous anymore. He felt important."

Poem: "The Butterfly" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) A caterpillar

"Once I was a caterpillar."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Leaves

"I munched on leaves all day long."

Q8Correct Answer: B) hour

"Hour" rhymes with "flower."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A New Year celebration

Fireworks, midnight, New Year's Eve = New Year celebration.

Q10Correct Answer: B) A fish would be a great first pet!...

Persuasive writing with benefits (easy to care for, learn responsibility).

Q11Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2

Camping: set up tents (3), cooked dinner (4), sang songs (1), slept (2).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Crickets are interesting insects known for their chirping.

Topic sentence about crickets matches the paragraph content.

Q13Correct Answer: B) You are making a mistake or wrong assumption

"Barking up the wrong tree" = making a wrong assumption.

Q14Correct Answer: B) They are like nature's paintings

"Grandfather says it's nature's way of painting the sky."

Q15Correct Answer: C) I am going to Ali's house.

Correct capitalization and apostrophe for possession.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It teaches teamwork and makes family life easier.

Connects to the example about finishing faster when everyone helps.

Reading Passage: "At the North Pole" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: C) A polar bear

White fur, huge paws, hunts seals, North Pole = polar bear.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator has white fur, huge paws, hunts seals, and lives at the North Pole

Classic polar bear characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) What is a snake's favorite subject? Hissstory!

A pun on "hiss" (snake sound) and "history."

Q20Correct Answer: B) Let me try to explain it to you. Maybe we can figure it out together.

Helpful and supportive response to a classmate's difficulty.
