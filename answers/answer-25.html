Reading Passage: "The New Baby" (Q1-5)

Haris was excited when Mom and Dad brought home his new baby sister, Zoya. But after a few days, he started feeling different.

Everyone paid attention to Zoya. Visitors brought gifts for her, not him. Mom and Dad seemed too busy to play with Haris.

"I wish she would go back," Haris thought one night.

Then one morning, Zoya grabbed Haris's finger and smiled at him. It was her first real smile!

"She smiled at me!" Haris shouted happily.

"Of course," said Mom. "She loves her big brother."

Suddenly, Haris did not feel jealous anymore. He felt important.

Q1 What new member joined Haris's family?
Correct Answer: B) A baby sister
"Mom and Dad brought home his new baby sister, Zoya."
Q2 Why did Haris start feeling unhappy?
Correct Answer: B) Everyone paid attention to the new baby
"Everyone paid attention to Zoya. Visitors brought gifts for her, not him."
Q3 What did Haris wish would happen?
Correct Answer: B) The baby would go back
"I wish she would go back," Haris thought.
Q4 What made Haris feel better?
Correct Answer: B) The baby smiled at him for the first time
"Zoya grabbed Haris's finger and smiled at him. It was her first real smile!"
Q5 How did Haris feel at the end?
Correct Answer: B) Important and happy
"Haris did not feel jealous anymore. He felt important."

Poem: "The Butterfly" (Q6-8)

Once I was a caterpillar,
Crawling on the ground.
I munched on leaves all day long,
Making crunchy sounds.

Then I wrapped myself up tight,
In a cozy cocoon bed.
When I woke up one fine day,
Beautiful wings I spread!

Q6 What was the narrator before becoming a butterfly?
Correct Answer: B) A caterpillar
"Once I was a caterpillar."
Q7 What did the caterpillar eat?
Correct Answer: C) Leaves
"I munched on leaves all day long."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Now I fly from flower to flower, Every minute, every ___________."
Correct Answer: B) hour
"Hour" rhymes with "flower."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? (Fireworks, midnight, New Year's Eve)
Correct Answer: B) A New Year celebration
Fireworks, midnight, New Year's Eve = New Year celebration.
Q10 You want to persuade your parents to let you have a pet fish. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) A fish would be a great first pet! It is easy to care for and I can learn about responsibility.
Persuasive writing with benefits (easy to care for, learn responsibility).
Q11 Put the sentences in order (camping story)
Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2
Camping: set up tents (3), cooked dinner (4), sang songs (1), slept (2).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence (paragraph about crickets)
Correct Answer: B) Crickets are interesting insects known for their chirping.
Topic sentence about crickets matches the paragraph content.
Q13 What does "barking up the wrong tree" mean?
Correct Answer: B) You are making a mistake or wrong assumption
"Barking up the wrong tree" = making a wrong assumption.
Q14 What does Grandfather think about sunsets?
Correct Answer: B) They are like nature's paintings
"Grandfather says it's nature's way of painting the sky."
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) I am going to Ali's house.
Correct capitalization and apostrophe for possession.
Q16 Which reason fits best with the opinion and example? (Helping with chores)
Correct Answer: B) It teaches teamwork and makes family life easier.
Connects to the example about finishing faster when everyone helps.

Reading Passage: "At the North Pole" (Q17-18)

Crunch, crunch. My huge paws break through the thin ice. I am searching for my favorite food - seals. My white fur keeps me warm and helps me hide in the snow.

Winter is the best time for hunting because the ice is thick. In summer, when the ice melts, finding food is much harder. But I am the largest land predator. I will survive!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: C) A polar bear
White fur, huge paws, hunts seals, North Pole = polar bear.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator has white fur, huge paws, hunts seals, and lives at the North Pole
Classic polar bear characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about school. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What is a snake's favorite subject? Hissstory!
A pun on "hiss" (snake sound) and "history."
Q20 Choose the best reply: Classmate says "I do not understand this math problem."
Correct Answer: B) Let me try to explain it to you. Maybe we can figure it out together.
Helpful and supportive response to a classmate's difficulty.
