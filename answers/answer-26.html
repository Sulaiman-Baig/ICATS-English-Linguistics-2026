Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Magic Paintbrush" (Q1-5)

Omar found an old paintbrush in his grandfather's attic. When he painted a picture of an apple, something amazing happened - the apple became real!

"This must be a magic paintbrush!" Omar exclaimed.

At first, Omar painted toys and candy. But then he saw a news report about children who had no food.

Omar thought carefully. He painted plates full of healthy food and delivered them to the shelter.

"Thank you!" the children said, their eyes shining with happiness.

Omar realized that the magic paintbrush was best used to help others, not just himself.

Q1 Where did Omar find the paintbrush?
Correct Answer: C) In his grandfather's attic
"Omar found an old paintbrush in his grandfather's attic."
Q2 What was special about the paintbrush?
Correct Answer: C) Whatever was painted became real
"When he painted a picture of an apple... the apple became real!"
Q3 What did Omar paint first?
Correct Answer: B) Toys and candy
"At first, Omar painted toys and candy."
Q4 What made Omar decide to help others?
Correct Answer: B) He saw a news report about hungry children
"He saw a news report about children who had no food."
Q5 What did Omar learn about the magic paintbrush?
Correct Answer: B) It was best used to help others
"The magic paintbrush was best used to help others, not just himself."

Poem: "My Best Friend" (Q6-8)

My best friend is always there,
Showing me that she does care.
We play together, laugh, and run,
Being friends is so much fun!

When I am sad, she cheers me up,
Like hot cocoa in a cup.
I am so lucky, can not you see,
To have a friend who's there for me!

Q6 What does the friend do when the speaker is sad?
Correct Answer: B) Cheers the speaker up
"When I am sad, she cheers me up."
Q7 What is the friend compared to?
Correct Answer: C) Hot cocoa in a cup
"She cheers me up, Like hot cocoa in a cup."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Friends like you are hard to find, You are so sweet and very ___________."
Correct Answer: C) kind
"Kind" rhymes with "find."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? (Red velvet seats, stage curtains, programs)
Correct Answer: B) A theater for live performances
Red velvet seats, stage curtains, programs = live theater.
Q10 You want to persuade your mom to make your favorite meal. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) I did really well on my test today. Could we celebrate with biryani for dinner? It would mean so much!
Persuasive writing with reason and emotional appeal.
Q11 Put the sentences in order (school race story)
Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 4, 1
Race: signed up (2), trained (3), race day (4), finished (1).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence (paragraph about beavers)
Correct Answer: B) Beavers are amazing builders.
Topic sentence about beavers matches the paragraph content.
Q13 What does "a penny for your thoughts" mean?
Correct Answer: B) What are you thinking about?
"A penny for your thoughts" = asking what someone is thinking.
Q14 Why did the family decide to eat at the park?
Correct Answer: B) Because the weather was beautiful
"The sun was shining... What a perfect day!"
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) Ms. Khan is our favorite teacher.
Correct capitalization and punctuation with title.
Q16 Which reason fits best with the opinion and example? (Volunteering)
Correct Answer: B) It makes you feel good to help others.
Connects to the example about feeling proud at the animal shelter.

Reading Passage: "Under the Shell" (Q17-18)

When danger comes, I pull in my head and legs. Safe inside my shell! I am slow, but I am protected. My house goes everywhere with me.

I love basking in the sun on a warm rock. Then I slide into the pond to cool off and find some tasty plants to eat. Life is good when you take it slow!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A turtle
Shell, slow, basks in sun, swims in pond = turtle.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator has a shell, is slow, basks in the sun, and swims in a pond
Classic turtle characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about cars. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Why do cars never get cold? Because they have their own heaters!
A joke playing on car heaters.
Q20 Choose the best reply: Dentist asks "Have you been brushing twice a day?"
Correct Answer: B) Yes, I brush every morning and night just like you said.
Polite and positive response to dentist.
