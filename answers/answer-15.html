Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Class Pet" (Q1-5)

Ms. Fatima's class had a new pet - a hamster named Peanut. The children took turns feeding him and cleaning his cage. Peanut loved running on his wheel at night.

One morning, the cage door was open, and Peanut was gone! The class looked everywhere - in the book corner, under the desks, behind the cabinets. No Peanut.

Then, little Amina heard a soft sound coming from her backpack. She opened it carefully, and there was Peanut, curled up in her lunchbox!

"He probably smelled your sandwich," laughed Ms. Fatima.

From that day on, everyone made sure to lock the cage door tightly. And Amina always checked her bag before leaving school!

Q1 What kind of pet did the class have?
Correct Answer: C) A hamster
"Ms. Fatima's class had a new pet - a hamster named Peanut."
Q2 What did Peanut love to do at night?
Correct Answer: C) Run on his wheel
"Peanut loved running on his wheel at night."
Q3 Where did the class look for Peanut?
Correct Answer: B) In the book corner, under desks, and behind cabinets
"The class looked everywhere - in the book corner, under the desks, behind the cabinets."
Q4 Where was Peanut found?
Correct Answer: B) In Amina's lunchbox in her backpack
"There was Peanut, curled up in her lunchbox!"
Q5 What did the class learn from this experience?
Correct Answer: B) Always lock the cage door properly
"From that day on, everyone made sure to lock the cage door tightly."

Poem: "Bedtime" (Q6-8)

The stars come out, the moon appears,
Time for sleep, my little dears.
Brush your teeth and say goodnight,
Everything will be alright.

Snuggle deep beneath the sheets,
Dream of all your favorite treats.
Tomorrow brings a brand new day,
With lots of time to run and play.

Q6 What time of day is this poem about?
Correct Answer: C) Bedtime / Night
"The stars come out, the moon appears, Time for sleep."
Q7 What should you do before sleeping according to the poem?
Correct Answer: B) Brush your teeth
"Brush your teeth and say goodnight."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Close your eyes and count some sheep, Soon you will be fast ___________."
Correct Answer: C) asleep
"Asleep" rhymes with "sheep" and fits the bedtime context.

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "The pharmacist handed over the medicine bottle. Behind her were shelves full of colorful boxes and bottles. A small line of customers waited at the counter."
Correct Answer: A) A pharmacy/drugstore
Pharmacist, medicine bottles, colorful boxes = pharmacy.
Q10 You want to persuade your dad to take you to the park. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Can we please go to the park? The weather is perfect and I need fresh air and exercise!
Persuasive - gives reasons (perfect weather, need for exercise).
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, I waited for the bus with my friends. 2. First, I woke up and got dressed. 3. Next, I ate breakfast and brushed my teeth. 4. Finally, the bus arrived and I went to school.
Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 1, 4
Morning routine: First woke up (2), ate breakfast (3), waited for bus (1), finally went to school (4).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. Some people use them to get to work every day. Bicycles do not need gasoline and do not cause pollution. Riding a bike is also great exercise."
Correct Answer: B) Bicycles are a great way to travel.
Topic sentence about bicycles matches the paragraph about bike benefits.
Q13 What does "bite off more than you can chew" mean? "By joining five clubs, Sarah bit off more than she could chew."
Correct Answer: B) Sarah took on more than she can handle
"Bite off more than you can chew" = taking on too many responsibilities.
Q14 Why were the farmers happy about the rain? "The morning started bright and sunny. But by afternoon, dark clouds rolled in and rain began to fall. The farmers were happy because their crops needed water."
Correct Answer: B) Because their crops needed water
"The farmers were happy because their crops needed water."
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: D) Dr. Ahmed is a great doctor.
Correct: "Dr." with period, proper capitalization, ends with period.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Breakfast is the most important meal. _______. For example, eating breakfast helps me concentrate better in class."
Correct Answer: B) It gives your body energy to start the day.
Connects breakfast to energy and concentration.

Reading Passage: "The Garden Helper" (Q17-18)

Bzzzz! I flew from flower to flower, collecting sweet nectar. The garden was full of colorful blooms today. While I work, I spread pollen that helps new flowers grow.

My hive is waiting for me to return. The other workers and I will make delicious honey from all this nectar. The queen will be pleased!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A bee
Flies, collects nectar, has a hive, makes honey, serves a queen = bee.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator flies, collects nectar, has a hive, and makes honey
All these are bee characteristics mentioned in the passage.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about computers. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Why was the computer cold? It left its Windows open!
A pun on "Windows" (the operating system vs. actual windows).
Q20 Choose the best reply: Parent: "Why did you not tell me about the field trip?" Child: ___
Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, I forgot. The permission slip is due tomorrow.
Polite apology with helpful information.
