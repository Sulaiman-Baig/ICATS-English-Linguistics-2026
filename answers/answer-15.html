Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Class Pet" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: C) A hamster

"Ms. Fatima's class had a new pet - a hamster named Peanut."

Q2Correct Answer: C) Run on his wheel

"Peanut loved running on his wheel at night."

Q3Correct Answer: B) In the book corner, under desks, and behind cabinets

"The class looked everywhere - in the book corner, under the desks, behind the cabinets."

Q4Correct Answer: B) In Amina's lunchbox in her backpack

"There was Peanut, curled up in her lunchbox!"

Q5Correct Answer: B) Always lock the cage door properly

"From that day on, everyone made sure to lock the cage door tightly."

Poem: "Bedtime" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: C) Bedtime / Night

"The stars come out, the moon appears, Time for sleep."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Brush your teeth

"Brush your teeth and say goodnight."

Q8Correct Answer: C) asleep

"Asleep" rhymes with "sheep" and fits the bedtime context.

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A pharmacy/drugstore

Pharmacist, medicine bottles, colorful boxes = pharmacy.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Can we please go to the park?...

Persuasive - gives reasons (perfect weather, need for exercise).

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 1, 4

Morning routine: First woke up (2), ate breakfast (3), waited for bus (1), finally went to school (4).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Bicycles are a great way to travel.

Topic sentence about bicycles matches the paragraph about bike benefits.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Sarah took on more than she can handle

"Bite off more than you can chew" = taking on too many responsibilities.

Q14Correct Answer: B) Because their crops needed water

"The farmers were happy because their crops needed water."

Q15Correct Answer: D) Dr. Ahmed is a great doctor.

Correct: "Dr." with period, proper capitalization, ends with period.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It gives your body energy to start the day.

Connects breakfast to energy and concentration.

Reading Passage: "The Garden Helper" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A bee

Flies, collects nectar, has a hive, makes honey, serves a queen = bee.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator flies, collects nectar, has a hive, and makes honey

All these are bee characteristics mentioned in the passage.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why was the computer cold? It left its Windows open!

A pun on "Windows" (the operating system vs. actual windows).

Q20Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, I forgot. The permission slip is due tomorrow.

Polite apology with helpful information.
