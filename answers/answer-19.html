Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Birthday Surprise" (Q1-5)

Maryam was turning seven years old. She woke up expecting a big celebration, but the house was quiet. "Happy birthday," Mom said calmly, giving her a small hug.

At breakfast, no one mentioned a party. At school, her friends seemed normal. Maryam felt disappointed. "Did everyone forget?" she wondered.

When she came home, the house was dark. Suddenly, the lights turned on! "SURPRISE!" everyone shouted. Her whole family and all her friends were there! Balloons, streamers, and a giant chocolate cake filled the room.

Maryam's eyes filled with happy tears. "This is the best surprise ever!"

Q1 How did Maryam feel when she woke up?
Correct Answer: B) Excited and expectant
"She woke up expecting a big celebration."
Q2 Why did Maryam feel disappointed during the day?
Correct Answer: B) No one seemed to remember her birthday
"Did everyone forget?" she wondered.
Q3 What was the surprise?
Correct Answer: C) A surprise birthday party
"SURPRISE!" with family, friends, balloons, and cake.
Q4 What kind of cake did she have?
Correct Answer: C) Chocolate
"A giant chocolate cake filled the room."
Q5 How did Maryam feel at the end?
Correct Answer: C) Extremely happy
"Maryam's eyes filled with happy tears."

Poem: "My Umbrella" (Q6-8)

Pitter, patter goes the rain,
Tapping softly on my windowpane.
I grab my umbrella, bright and blue,
And splash in puddles, one and two!

The umbrella keeps me nice and dry,
While raindrops fall down from the sky.
I love the rain, I love to play,
Every rainy day is a special day!

Q6 What sound does the rain make in the poem?
Correct Answer: B) Pitter, patter
"Pitter, patter goes the rain."
Q7 What color is the umbrella?
Correct Answer: C) Blue
"I grab my umbrella, bright and blue."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "After the rain, the sun comes out, And children play and run ___________."
Correct Answer: B) about
"About" rhymes with "out."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "The cashier scanned the groceries while Mom put them in bags. The store was crowded with people pushing carts full of food and supplies."
Correct Answer: B) A grocery store/supermarket
Cashier, groceries, carts = supermarket.
Q10 You want to persuade your sibling to play a game with you. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Let us play a game together! It will be so much fun and we can take turns picking what to play!
Persuasive with enthusiasm and fairness.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, we put the tent up. 2. Finally, we roasted marshmallows by the fire. 3. First, we drove to the campsite. 4. Next, we collected firewood.
Correct Answer: A) 3, 1, 4, 2
Camping: drove there (3), set up tent (1), collected wood (4), roasted marshmallows (2).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. First, you need to find a safe place to cross. Look left, then right, then left again. Wait for all cars to stop or for the signal to turn green. Walk, do not run, across the street."
Correct Answer: B) Here is how to cross the street safely.
Topic sentence matches the safety instructions.
Q13 What does "a blessing in disguise" mean? "Missing the bus was a blessing in disguise because I found my lost wallet while walking."
Correct Answer: B) Something bad that turns out to be good
"A blessing in disguise" = unexpected positive outcome.
Q14 How did Dad respond to Ali's situation? "Tears rolled down Ali's face as his ice cream fell to the ground. Dad saw his sad face and took him back to the ice cream shop. This time, Ali held his cone very carefully!"
Correct Answer: B) He kindly bought Ali a new ice cream
Dad took him back to the ice cream shop.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) I have a cat named Fluffy.
Correct capitalization of "I" and "Fluffy" (proper noun).
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Playing outside is better than watching TV. _______. For example, you can run, climb, and breathe fresh air when you play outside."
Correct Answer: B) It keeps your body active and healthy.
Connects outdoor play to physical activity.

Reading Passage: "In the Beehive" (Q17-18)

Buzz, buzz! Another busy day in the hive. I flew out at sunrise to collect nectar from the flowers in the meadow. My sisters and I work together to make honey for our queen and the whole colony.

When I found a patch of flowers, I did a special dance to tell the others where to find them. Communication is important when you have thousands of family members!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A worker bee
Collects nectar, works with sisters, serves the queen = worker bee.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator collects nectar, makes honey, dances to communicate, and lives in a hive
All worker bee characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about dogs. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What do you call a dog that does magic tricks? A Labracadabrador!
A pun combining "Labrador" and "abracadabra."
Q20 Choose the best reply: Neighbor: "I am looking for my cat. Have you seen it?" You: ___
Correct Answer: B) What does it look like? I will help you look for it.
Helpful and caring response.
