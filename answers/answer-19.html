Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Birthday Surprise" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) Excited and expectant

"She woke up expecting a big celebration."

Q2Correct Answer: B) No one seemed to remember her birthday

"Did everyone forget?" she wondered.

Q3Correct Answer: C) A surprise birthday party

"SURPRISE!" with family, friends, balloons, and cake.

Q4Correct Answer: C) Chocolate

"A giant chocolate cake filled the room."

Q5Correct Answer: C) Extremely happy

"Maryam's eyes filled with happy tears."

Poem: "My Umbrella" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) Pitter, patter

"Pitter, patter goes the rain."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Blue

"I grab my umbrella, bright and blue."

Q8Correct Answer: B) about

"About" rhymes with "out."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A grocery store/supermarket

Cashier, groceries, carts = supermarket.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Let us play a game together!...

Persuasive with enthusiasm and fairness.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 3, 1, 4, 2

Camping: drove there (3), set up tent (1), collected wood (4), roasted marshmallows (2).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Here is how to cross the street safely.

Topic sentence matches the safety instructions.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Something bad that turns out to be good

"A blessing in disguise" = unexpected positive outcome.

Q14Correct Answer: B) He kindly bought Ali a new ice cream

Dad took him back to the ice cream shop.

Q15Correct Answer: B) I have a cat named Fluffy.

Correct capitalization of "I" and "Fluffy" (proper noun).

Q16Correct Answer: B) It keeps your body active and healthy.

Connects outdoor play to physical activity.

Reading Passage: "In the Beehive" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A worker bee

Collects nectar, works with sisters, serves the queen = worker bee.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator collects nectar, makes honey, dances to communicate, and lives in a hive

All worker bee characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) What do you call a dog that does magic tricks? A Labracadabrador!

A pun combining "Labrador" and "abracadabra."

Q20Correct Answer: B) What does it look like? I will help you look for it.

Helpful and caring response.
