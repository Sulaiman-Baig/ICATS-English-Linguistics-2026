Reading Passage: "The Snowball Fight" (Q1-5)

Winter had finally arrived! Sameer woke up to find his backyard covered in white, fluffy snow. He quickly put on his warmest clothes and ran outside.

His neighbor, Hina, was already building a snow fort. "Want to have a snowball fight?" she called out.

"You are on!" Sameer shouted back.

They spent the whole morning throwing snowballs and laughing. When they got cold, Sameer's mom called them inside for hot chocolate with marshmallows.

"This is the best winter day ever!" Sameer said, warming his hands on the mug.

Q1 What did Sameer see when he woke up?
Correct Answer: B) His backyard covered in snow
"Sameer woke up to find his backyard covered in white, fluffy snow."
Q2 What was Hina doing when Sameer came outside?
Correct Answer: B) Building a snow fort
"His neighbor, Hina, was already building a snow fort."
Q3 What did they do when they got cold?
Correct Answer: B) Went home and drank hot chocolate
"When they got cold, Sameer's mom called them inside for hot chocolate."
Q4 What was in the hot chocolate?
Correct Answer: C) Marshmallows
"Hot chocolate with marshmallows."
Q5 How did Sameer feel about the day?
Correct Answer: B) It was the best winter day ever
"This is the best winter day ever!"

Poem: "My Pet Goldfish" (Q6-8)

In a bowl of crystal clear,
My little goldfish swims so near.
Round and round she goes all day,
In her home where she loves to play.

Golden scales that shine so bright,
She sparkles in the morning light.
I tap the glass to say hello,
She wiggles her fins and says "hello" right back, you know!

Q6 Where does the goldfish live?
Correct Answer: B) In a crystal clear bowl
"In a bowl of crystal clear."
Q7 What color are the fish's scales?
Correct Answer: C) Golden
"Golden scales that shine so bright."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "I feed her every single day, And watch her swim and splash and ___________."
Correct Answer: B) play
"Play" rhymes with "day."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "The pilot announced they were about to land. Passengers buckled their seatbelts. Through the small windows, they could see clouds below them."
Correct Answer: C) An airplane
Pilot, passengers, seatbelts, clouds below = airplane.
Q10 You want to persuade your friend to exercise more. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Exercise helps you stay healthy, gives you more energy, and is fun when we do it together!
Persuasive - gives benefits of exercise.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, I added colorful sprinkles on top. 2. Finally, I gave it to my friend as a gift. 3. First, I baked a batch of cupcakes. 4. Next, I spread pink frosting on each one.
Correct Answer: A) 3, 4, 1, 2
First baked cupcakes (3), spread frosting (4), added sprinkles (1), finally gave as gift (2).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. They can carry messages across long distances very quickly. Unlike letters, emails arrive almost instantly. You can also send pictures and videos through email."
Correct Answer: B) Emails are a fast way to communicate.
Topic sentence about emails matches the paragraph about email benefits.
Q13 What does "spill the beans" mean? "Please do not spill the beans about the surprise party!"
Correct Answer: B) Tell the secret
"Spill the beans" = reveal a secret.
Q14 Why did the teacher let the students play a game? "The students finished their class work quickly and correctly. As a reward, their teacher let them play a fun word game for the last fifteen minutes."
Correct Answer: B) Because they finished their work well
"As a reward" for finishing quickly and correctly.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) Do you want to play soccer?
Correct capitalization and question mark.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Summer is the best season. _______. For example, we can go swimming and have picnics in the park."
Correct Answer: B) There are many fun outdoor activities to enjoy in summer.
Connects summer to fun activities like swimming and picnics.

Reading Passage: "Underground Home" (Q17-18)

I pushed the dirt with my strong front legs. Digging tunnels is what I do best! My home has many rooms - one for sleeping, one for storing food, and even a bathroom far from the others.

Above ground, the farmer grows carrots. Sometimes I sneak up and grab one for a snack. Delicious! Then I scurry back underground where it is safe and cool.

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A rabbit or mole/groundhog
Digs tunnels, lives underground, eats carrots = rabbit or groundhog.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator digs tunnels, lives underground, and eats carrots
All these are characteristics of a burrowing animal.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about weather. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What did one raindrop say to the other? Two is company, three is a cloud!
A pun on the saying "two's company, three's a crowd" applied to weather.
Q20 Choose the best reply: Coach: "Great job at practice today!" Player: ___
Correct Answer: B) Thank you, Coach! I have been working hard.
Polite and humble response acknowledging the coach's compliment.
