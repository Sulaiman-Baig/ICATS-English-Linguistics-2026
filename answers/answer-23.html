Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Rainy Day Plan" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) It was raining too hard

"The field trip is cancelled... It is raining too hard."

Q2Correct Answer: B) Disappointed

"The class groaned" shows they were disappointed.

Q3Correct Answer: B) Created a classroom zoo activity

"We are going to have a zoo in our classroom instead!"

Q4Correct Answer: B) Researched animals and made presentations

"Each group researched a different animal and made a presentation."

Q5Correct Answer: B) It was even better than the real zoo

"This was even better than the real zoo!" said Kamran.

Poem: "My Family" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) A garden

"My family is like a garden."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Mom

"Mom is like the sunshine bright."

Q8Correct Answer: B) grow

"Grow" rhymes with "so."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A swimming pool/water park

Water slides, lifeguards, sunscreen = water park setting.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Try this samosa!...

Persuasive writing with enthusiasm and benefits.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3

Gas station: drove there (2), filled gas (4), car wash (1), ice cream (3).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Soups are delicious and comforting meals.

Topic sentence about soups matches the paragraph content.

Q13Correct Answer: B) You were exactly right

"Hit the nail on the head" = being exactly correct.

Q14Correct Answer: B) Consistent practice leads to improvement

Sara practiced daily and improved from barely playing to beautiful songs.

Q15Correct Answer: B) January is the first month of the year.

Correct capitalization and punctuation.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It helps you remember your thoughts and see your progress.

Connects to the example about seeing growth over time.

Reading Passage: "The Queen of the Hive" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A queen bee

Lays eggs, stays in hive, workers care for her = queen bee.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator lays eggs, does not leave the hive, and is cared for by workers

Classic queen bee characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) What did the cloud say to the lightning bolt? You are shocking!

A pun on "shocking" (surprising and electrical).

Q20Correct Answer: B) I am so happy! All my studying paid off!

Positive and grateful response to good news.
