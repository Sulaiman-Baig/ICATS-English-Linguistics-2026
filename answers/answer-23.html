Reading Passage: "The Rainy Day Plan" (Q1-5)

"The field trip is cancelled," announced Ms. Naheed. "It is raining too hard."

The class groaned. They had been looking forward to visiting the zoo for weeks.

"But," Ms. Naheed continued with a smile, "we are going to have a zoo in our classroom instead!"

She divided the class into groups. Each group researched a different animal and made a presentation. They drew pictures, shared facts, and even made animal sounds!

By the end of the day, everyone was laughing and learning. "This was even better than the real zoo!" said Kamran.

Q1 Why was the field trip cancelled?
Correct Answer: B) It was raining too hard
"The field trip is cancelled... It is raining too hard."
Q2 How did the students feel when they heard the news?
Correct Answer: B) Disappointed
"The class groaned" shows they were disappointed.
Q3 What did Ms. Naheed do instead of the field trip?
Correct Answer: B) Created a classroom zoo activity
"We are going to have a zoo in our classroom instead!"
Q4 What did the groups do?
Correct Answer: B) Researched animals and made presentations
"Each group researched a different animal and made a presentation."
Q5 How did Kamran feel about the classroom activity?
Correct Answer: B) It was even better than the real zoo
"This was even better than the real zoo!" said Kamran.

Poem: "My Family" (Q6-8)

My family is like a garden,
Each one special, each one fun.
Mom is like the sunshine bright,
Dad is strong, he holds us tight.

Sister is a pretty flower,
Brother makes me laugh each hour.
Grandma tells the best stories,
Grandpa shares the morning glories.

Q6 What is the family compared to in the poem?
Correct Answer: B) A garden
"My family is like a garden."
Q7 Who is compared to sunshine?
Correct Answer: C) Mom
"Mom is like the sunshine bright."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "We all love each other so, Together we learn and ___________."
Correct Answer: B) grow
"Grow" rhymes with "so."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "Water splashed everywhere as kids went down the slides. Lifeguards in red shirts watched from tall chairs. The smell of sunscreen was in the air."
Correct Answer: A) A swimming pool/water park
Water slides, lifeguards, sunscreen = water park setting.
Q10 You want to persuade your friend to try a new food. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Try this samosa! It is crispy on the outside and has yummy potatoes inside. You will love it!
Persuasive writing with enthusiasm and benefits.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Next, we went through the car wash. 2. First, Dad drove the car to the gas station. 3. Finally, we got ice cream to celebrate our clean car. 4. Then, we filled up the gas tank.
Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3
Gas station: drove there (2), filled gas (4), car wash (1), ice cream (3).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. They include meat, vegetables, and sometimes rice or noodles. Soups can be thin or thick. On a cold day, a warm bowl of soup is perfect."
Correct Answer: B) Soups are delicious and comforting meals.
Topic sentence about soups matches the paragraph content.
Q13 What does "hit the nail on the head" mean? "You hit the nail on the head with your answer!"
Correct Answer: B) You were exactly right
"Hit the nail on the head" = being exactly correct.
Q14 What is the main message? "Six months ago, Sara could barely play a simple tune on the piano. But she practiced every single day, even when she didn't feel like it. Now she can play beautiful songs that make her family smile."
Correct Answer: B) Consistent practice leads to improvement
Sara practiced daily and improved from barely playing to beautiful songs.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) January is the first month of the year.
Correct capitalization and punctuation.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Keeping a diary is a good habit. _______. For example, I can look back and see how much I have grown since last year."
Correct Answer: B) It helps you remember your thoughts and see your progress.
Connects to the example about seeing growth over time.

Reading Passage: "The Queen of the Hive" (Q17-18)

Every day, thousands of my daughters bring me food. I am the most important member of our home. Without me, there would be no new bees to collect honey.

I do not leave the hive much anymore. My job is to lay eggs - hundreds every day! The workers take care of everything else. It is good to be the queen!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A queen bee
Lays eggs, stays in hive, workers care for her = queen bee.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator lays eggs, does not leave the hive, and is cared for by workers
Classic queen bee characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about the sky. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What did the cloud say to the lightning bolt? You are shocking!
A pun on "shocking" (surprising and electrical).
Q20 Choose the best reply: Parent: "You got an A on your spelling test!" Child: ___
Correct Answer: B) I am so happy! All my studying paid off!
Positive and grateful response to good news.
