Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Lost Teddy Bear" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: C) Mr. Buttons

"Ayesha loved her teddy bear, Mr. Buttons."

Q2Correct Answer: B) At the mall

"After a trip to the mall, Mr. Buttons was missing!"

Q3Correct Answer: B) It had a name tag with her name

"We found a teddy bear with a name tag that says Ayesha."

Q4Correct Answer: C) Very sad

"She cried herself to sleep that night."

Q5Correct Answer: B) Name tags on belongings can help return lost items

The name tag helped security return the teddy bear.

Poem: "Grandma's Cookies" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) Chocolate chip cookies

"Chocolate chips in every bite."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Stir the dough

"She lets me help her stir the dough."

Q8Correct Answer: B) tea

"Tea" rhymes with "me."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A park playground

Slides, swings, children playing, parents watching = playground.

Q10Correct Answer: B) A bicycle would help me exercise...

Persuasive with benefits (exercise, visiting friends).

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 4, 1

Letter: wrote (2), envelope (3), stamp (4), mailbox (1).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Flashlights are useful tools.

Topic sentence about flashlights matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Do not bring up an old problem that might cause trouble

"Let sleeping dogs lie" = avoid stirring up old issues.

Q14Correct Answer: B) They can find fun in any situation

They had a car picnic and laughed about their adventure.

Q15Correct Answer: C) Can you pass me the salt, please?

Correct capitalization, comma before "please," question mark.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It helps you communicate with more people.

Connects to example of talking to grandmother.

Reading Passage: "Morning Swim" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A penguin

Slides on belly, catches fish, South Pole = penguin.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator slides on its belly, dives for fish, has fluffy babies, and lives at the South Pole

Classic penguin characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the banana go to the doctor? Because it was not peeling well!

A pun on "peeling" (banana skin) and "feeling."

Q20Correct Answer: B) Sure! What do you need help with?

Helpful and friendly response.
