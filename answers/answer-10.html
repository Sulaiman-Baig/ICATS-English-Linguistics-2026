Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Giving Tree" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: C) Mangoes and shade

The tree "gave sweet mangoes" and "elders rested in its shade."

Q2Correct Answer: B) No rain and the river dried up

"One hot summer, no rain came. The river dried up."

Q3Correct Answer: B) It dropped extra leaves and gave more fruit

The tree "dropped extra leaves to cover the ground and keep it cool. It gave more fruit than ever before."

Q4Correct Answer: C) They decorated it with ribbons and flowers

"They decorated it with colorful ribbons and flowers."

Q5Correct Answer: B) Helping others brings happiness

"As long as I can give, I am happy" - the tree finds happiness in giving.

Poem: "My Bicycle" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) Free and happy

"Feeling free" and "Oh, what fun it is for me!"

Q7Correct Answer: B) Past shops and through the park

"Past the shops and through the park"

Q8Correct Answer: B) sky

"Sky" rhymes with "by" in "I ring my bell as I go by."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A dentist's office

Dentist, mouth wide open, bright light, tools on a tray - all describe a dentist's office.

Q10Correct Answer: B) With less homework, we would have more time to rest and play...

This is persuasive as it gives benefits of the suggestion.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 1, 3, 4

PB&J order: First (2) bread, Then (1) peanut butter, Next (3) jelly, Finally (4) together.

Q12Correct Answer: B) Here is how bees make honey.

The paragraph explains the process of honey-making.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Be quiet and stop talking

"Zip your lip" means to be quiet, like closing a zipper.

Q14Correct Answer: C) Finishing responsibilities first leads to rewards

Sarah finished her chores and homework, then got to play.

Q15Correct Answer: C) Wow! That was amazing!

Correct exclamation marks and capitalization.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It helps you make your own meals and be independent.

This connects the opinion with the example of making breakfast.

Reading Passage: "The Dance Performance" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A dancer performing on stage

The narrator leaped onto stage, twirled and jumped, and bowed at the end.

Q18Correct Answer: C) Happy and proud

"Tears of happiness" and feeling the hours of practice were "worth it."

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the pencil go to school? Because it wanted to be sharp!

Wordplay - "sharp" means both intelligent and having a sharp point (pencils need sharpening).

Q20Correct Answer: B) Thank you! I studied really hard.

A polite, humble response acknowledging the compliment and effort.
