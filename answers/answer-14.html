Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Paper Boat" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) During art class

"Rayan made a paper boat during art class."

Q2Correct Answer: B) He wanted to see it sail

He thought "Perfect!" and watched it sail away.

Q3Correct Answer: B) It disappeared

"The boat disappeared into the darkness."

Q4Correct Answer: B) It was in his garden

"I found it in my garden."

Q5Correct Answer: B) Sometimes things work out unexpectedly

The boat returned in an unexpected way.

Poem: "The Treehouse" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) In the branches of an old oak tree

"High up in the branches of the old oak tree."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Rooftops, the church, and Dad's car

"The rooftops, the church, even Dad's parked car."

Q8Correct Answer: B) gales

"Gales" rhymes with "tales."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: C) A farm

Farmer, cows, barn, chickens, tractor = farm.

Q10Correct Answer: B) With new art supplies, I can create beautiful drawings...

Persuasive - shows benefits.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 4, 1

Building: First (2) gathered, Then (3) sawed, Next (4) hammered, Finally (1) hung.

Q12Correct Answer: A) Dolphins are amazing animals that live in the sea.

Topic sentence about dolphins matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Never / something that will never happen

"When pigs fly" = never (pigs cannot fly).

Q14Correct Answer: B) They made it fun by telling stories

"They lit candles and told stories."

Q15Correct Answer: C) I can not wait to see the movie!

Correct capitalization and exclamation mark.

Q16Correct Answer: B) They help keep our air clean and protect wildlife.

Connects the opinion to the example about oxygen and birds.

Reading Passage: "My Home" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A turtle/tortoise

Shell, pulls head/legs in, slow, eats lettuce, lives in tank = turtle.

Q18Correct Answer: B) Pulls head and legs inside the shell

"I just pull my head and legs inside."

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the doctor carry a red pen? In case they needed to draw blood!

Wordplay on "draw blood" - drawing with pen vs medical procedure.

Q20Correct Answer: B) Yes, I will have the chicken sandwich, please.

Polite, direct answer to the question.
