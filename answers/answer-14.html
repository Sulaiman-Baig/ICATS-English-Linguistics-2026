Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Paper Boat" (Q1-5)

Rayan made a paper boat during art class. It was white with blue stripes, and he wrote his name on the side. He was very proud of it.

On his way home, it started to rain. Rayan saw a stream of water flowing down the street. "Perfect!" he thought. He placed his boat in the water and watched it sail away.

The boat floated past his house, past the park, and toward the storm drain. "Come back!" Rayan cried, but the boat disappeared into the darkness.

The next morning, Rayan was sad at breakfast. Then his neighbor, old Mr. Khan, knocked on the door. "Is this yours?" he asked, holding up the paper boat. "I found it in my garden. The wind must have blown it there!"

Rayan could not believe it. His boat had gone on quite an adventure!

Q1 Where did Rayan make the paper boat?
Correct Answer: B) During art class
"Rayan made a paper boat during art class."
Q2 Why did Rayan put his boat in the water?
Correct Answer: B) He wanted to see it sail
He thought "Perfect!" and watched it sail away.
Q3 What happened to the boat in the storm drain?
Correct Answer: B) It disappeared
"The boat disappeared into the darkness."
Q4 How did Mr. Khan find the boat?
Correct Answer: B) It was in his garden
"I found it in my garden."
Q5 What did Rayan learn from this experience?
Correct Answer: B) Sometimes things work out unexpectedly
The boat returned in an unexpected way.

Poem: "The Treehouse" (Q6-8)

High up in the branches of the old oak tree,
There sits a special house just for you and me.
We climb up the ladder, step by step with care,
Until we reach our secret spot in the air.

From our treehouse we can see so far,
The rooftops, the church, even Dad's parked car.
A place to dream and a place to play,
Our treehouse is the best part of my day.

Q6 Where is the treehouse located?
Correct Answer: B) In the branches of an old oak tree
"High up in the branches of the old oak tree."
Q7 What can they see from the treehouse?
Correct Answer: B) Rooftops, the church, and Dad's car
"The rooftops, the church, even Dad's parked car."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "We read our books and tell our tales, While wind blows through like gentle ___________."
Correct Answer: B) gales
"Gales" rhymes with "tales."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? The farmer milked the cows in the big red barn. Chickens wandered nearby, pecking at the ground. A tractor was parked in the field.
Correct Answer: C) A farm
Farmer, cows, barn, chickens, tractor = farm.
Q10 You want to persuade your mom to buy you art supplies. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) With new art supplies, I can create beautiful drawings and improve my skills!
Persuasive - shows benefits.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Finally, we hung the birdhouse in the tree. 2. First, we gathered wood and nails. 3. Then, Dad helped me saw the pieces. 4. Next, we hammered the pieces together.
Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 4, 1
Building: First (2) gathered, Then (3) sawed, Next (4) hammered, Finally (1) hung.
Q12 Read the paragraph and add the best topic sentence: ___. They are very smart and can learn many tricks. Dolphins communicate using clicks and whistles. They live in groups called pods and help take care of each other.
Correct Answer: A) Dolphins are amazing animals that live in the sea.
Topic sentence about dolphins matches the paragraph.
Q13 What does "when pigs fly" mean in: "I will clean my room when pigs fly," joked Aamir.
Correct Answer: B) Never / something that will never happen
"When pigs fly" = never (pigs cannot fly).
Q14 Lightning flashed across the sky, and thunder shook the windows. Then everything went dark - the power had gone out! The family lit candles and told stories until the lights came back on. How did the family handle the power outage?
Correct Answer: B) They made it fun by telling stories
"They lit candles and told stories."
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) I can not wait to see the movie!
Correct capitalization and exclamation mark.
Q16 Which reason fits best? Planting trees is good for the environment. ___. For example, trees give us oxygen and provide homes for birds.
Correct Answer: B) They help keep our air clean and protect wildlife.
Connects the opinion to the example about oxygen and birds.

Reading Passage: "My Home" (Q17-18)

I love my shell. It keeps me safe from predators and the hot sun. Whenever I feel scared, I just pull my head and legs inside.

I move slowly, but that is okay. I have nowhere to rush to. Today, I will munch on some lettuce and take a nap in my tank. What a perfect life!

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A turtle/tortoise
Shell, pulls head/legs in, slow, eats lettuce, lives in tank = turtle.
Q18 What does the narrator do when scared?
Correct Answer: B) Pulls head and legs inside the shell
"I just pull my head and legs inside."

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about doctors. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Why did the doctor carry a red pen? In case they needed to draw blood!
Wordplay on "draw blood" - drawing with pen vs medical procedure.
Q20 Choose the best reply: Waiter: Are you ready to order? Customer: ___
Correct Answer: B) Yes, I will have the chicken sandwich, please.
Polite, direct answer to the question.
