Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Thank You Project" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) Write a thank you letter

"Write a thank you letter to someone who has helped you."

Q2Correct Answer: C) The school janitor

"Zain decided to write to the school janitor, Mr. Riaz."

Q3Correct Answer: B) For keeping the school clean and being kind

"You always keep our school clean and smile at us every morning."

Q4Correct Answer: C) He had tears of happiness

"When Mr. Riaz received the letter, he had tears in his eyes."

Q5Correct Answer: B) Kindness matters, especially to those often overlooked

"Kindness matters, especially to those who often go unnoticed."

Poem: "My Schoolbag" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: C) Heavy

"My schoolbag is heavy, full of things."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Books, pencils, rulers, strings, erasers, crayons, scissors

The poem lists all these items inside the schoolbag.

Q8Correct Answer: B) grow

"Grow" rhymes with "go."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) An aquarium

Fish in glass tanks, shark swimming overhead, guide = aquarium.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Having class outside would help us learn about nature...

Persuasive writing with benefits and reasoning.

Q11Correct Answer: C) 2, 3, 4, 1

Space center: arrived (2), learned about astronauts (3), saw rockets (4), watched blast off (1).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Birds build amazing nests.

Topic sentence about bird nests matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Time seems to pass quickly

"Time flies" = time passes quickly.

Q14Correct Answer: B) It was very engaging and exciting

"She couldn't put it down" - finished 200 pages in one day.

Q15Correct Answer: C) Did you see the bird in the tree?

Correct capitalization and question mark for a question.

Q16Correct Answer: B) People trust and respect honest people.

Connects to the example about parents being proud.

Reading Passage: "Ocean Journey" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: C) A whale

Migrates, sings low sounds, mammal, longest journey = whale.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator migrates, sings in low sounds, is a mammal, and makes the longest journey

Classic whale characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why did the astronaut break up with his girlfriend? Because he needed more space!

A pun on "space" (room and outer space).

Q20Correct Answer: B) You are welcome! I wanted to help out.

Polite and humble response to thanks.
