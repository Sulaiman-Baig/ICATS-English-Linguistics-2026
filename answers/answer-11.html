Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Secret Garden Door" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) While playing hide and seek with her cat

"While playing hide and seek with her cat Whiskers, she discovered it."

Q2Correct Answer: C) A rusty key

"A rusty key hung from a nail nearby."

Q3Correct Answer: B) A beautiful secret garden

"The most beautiful garden she had ever seen" with butterflies, flowers, fountain.

Q4Correct Answer: C) Her grandmother when she was young

"This was my secret garden when I was your age."

Q5Correct Answer: C) Excited

"Emma's heart raced" shows excitement and anticipation.

Poem: "Morning Song" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) The rooster

"The rooster crows at break of day, Wake up, wake up."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Dewdrops

"The dewdrops sparkle on the grass."

Q8Correct Answer: C) still

"Still" rhymes with "hill" and fits "fresh and still" meaning calm.

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A bakery

Baker, white apron, tall hat, flour, eggs, butter, fresh bread smell = bakery.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Vegetables make you strong and give you energy to play!

Persuasive writing gives benefits to convince someone.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 3, 1

First (2) planted, Next (4) watered, Then (3) sprout, Finally (1) flower.

Q12Correct Answer: B) Octopuses are fascinating sea creatures.

Topic sentence introduces the main idea matching the details about octopuses.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Very rarely / not often

"Once in a blue moon" is an idiom meaning something happens rarely.

Q14Correct Answer: B) Because he was hungry

"Amir's stomach was growling" indicates he was hungry.

Q15Correct Answer: B) My dog likes to run, jump, and play.

Proper commas in list, capital letter, period at end.

Q16Correct Answer: C) There are so many fun things to do when it rains.

This explains why rainy days are the best, matching the puddle example.

Reading Passage: "Night Watch" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) An owl

Wings, big eyes for night vision, silent flight, hunts mice = owl.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator has wings, big eyes, flies silently, and hunts mice

All these characteristics together identify an owl.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) What did the ocean say to the beach? Nothing, it just waved!

A pun on "waved" - ocean waves and waving hello.

Q20Correct Answer: B) That is okay. We can celebrate together now!

A kind, understanding, and positive response to a friend's apology.
