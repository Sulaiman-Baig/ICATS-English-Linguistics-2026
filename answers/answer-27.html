Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Wrong Bus" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) It was his first day taking the bus alone

"It was Farhan's first day taking the bus alone."

Q2Correct Answer: B) He got on the wrong bus

"This is the Blue Route. You need the Red Route."

Q3Correct Answer: B) The streets looked unfamiliar

"Farhan noticed the streets looked unfamiliar."

Q4Correct Answer: B) She called the right bus and had it wait

"She called the other bus and told them to wait."

Q5Correct Answer: B) It is okay to make mistakes and ask for help

"Everyone makes mistakes. The important thing is asking for help."

Poem: "The Train" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) Choo, choo and click-clack

"Choo, choo" and "Click-clack on the tracks."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Past mountains and through plains

"Past the mountains, through the plains."

Q8Correct Answer: B) hear

"Hear" rhymes with "clear."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A bakery

Baker, fresh bread, oven, pastries, display case = bakery.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Join the book club with me!...

Persuasive with enthusiasm and benefits.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3

Vet visit: took cat (2), waited (4), check-up (1), went home (3).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Microscopes are important tools for science.

Topic sentence about microscopes matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Do not spoil my happiness

"Raining on someone's parade" = ruining their happiness.

Q14Correct Answer: B) It was worth it for the good food

"The delicious food made the wait worthwhile."

Q15Correct Answer: C) Wow! That is the biggest cake I have ever seen!

Correct exclamation marks and capitalization.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It keeps your body active and improves your mood.

Connects to example about feeling energetic after walking.

Reading Passage: "Life on Eight Legs" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: C) A spider

Eight legs, spins webs, eats flies = spider.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator has eight legs, spins webs, and eats flies

Classic spider characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) How does the man in the moon cut his hair? Eclipse it!

A pun on "eclipse" and "he clips it."

Q20Correct Answer: B) I am sure you will do great! Let us study together tonight.

Supportive and helpful response to a worried friend.
