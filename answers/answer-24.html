Reading Passage: "The Neighborhood Cleanup" (Q1-5)

Every Saturday, the Ahmed family had a tradition. They would spend one hour cleaning up their neighborhood.

Little Farhan thought it was boring at first. "Why do we have to pick up other people's trash?" he complained.

"Because this is our home too," Dad explained. "We all share this neighborhood."

After a few weeks, Farhan noticed something amazing. The streets were cleaner, flowers were blooming, and neighbors were thanking them. Some even joined them!

"This is not boring anymore," Farhan said with a smile. "This is awesome!"

Q1 What did the Ahmed family do every Saturday?
Correct Answer: B) Cleaned up their neighborhood
"Every Saturday, the Ahmed family... would spend one hour cleaning up their neighborhood."
Q2 How did Farhan feel about the cleanup at first?
Correct Answer: C) He thought it was boring
"Little Farhan thought it was boring at first."
Q3 What did Dad say about why they helped?
Correct Answer: B) The neighborhood is their shared home
"Because this is our home too... We all share this neighborhood."
Q4 What happened after a few weeks?
Correct Answer: B) Streets were cleaner and neighbors joined
"The streets were cleaner... neighbors were thanking them. Some even joined them!"
Q5 How did Farhan's feelings change by the end?
Correct Answer: C) He found it awesome
"This is not boring anymore... This is awesome!"

Poem: "The Night Sky" (Q6-8)

The stars are diamonds in the sky,
Twinkling brightly way up high.
The moon is like a silver boat,
Across the darkness it does float.

I make a wish upon a star,
Though I know it is so far.
The night sky fills me with such wonder,
As I listen to the thunder.

Q6 What are the stars compared to?
Correct Answer: B) Diamonds
"The stars are diamonds in the sky."
Q7 What is the moon compared to?
Correct Answer: C) A silver boat
"The moon is like a silver boat."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "Before I close my eyes tonight, I blow a kiss to stars so ___________."
Correct Answer: B) bright
"Bright" rhymes with "tonight."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "Vegetables and fruits were stacked in colorful rows. Farmers stood behind tables selling fresh produce. Customers walked around with baskets."
Correct Answer: B) A farmers market
Farmers selling fresh produce, colorful vegetables, customers with baskets = farmers market.
Q10 You want to persuade your class to help the environment. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) If we all work together to reduce, reuse, and recycle, we can keep our Earth clean for everyone!
Persuasive writing with action and benefits for everyone.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, I saw many colorful fish. 2. First, I put on my mask and snorkel. 3. Finally, I saw a beautiful coral reef. 4. Next, I swam into the water.
Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3
Snorkeling: mask on (2), swam in (4), saw fish (1), saw reef (3).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. Cheetahs can run up to 70 miles per hour. Dolphins are excellent swimmers. Even birds have special features that help them fly."
Correct Answer: B) Different animals have different amazing abilities.
Topic sentence matches examples of cheetahs running, dolphins swimming, birds flying.
Q13 What does "two peas in a pod" mean? "Ali and Ahmed are like two peas in a pod."
Correct Answer: B) They are very similar
"Two peas in a pod" = very alike or similar.
Q14 What lesson can Bilal learn? "Bilal left his umbrella at home even though his mother reminded him. When the rain started during recess, he had to wait under the school roof while his friends with umbrellas walked home."
Correct Answer: B) It's important to listen to good advice
Bilal didn't listen to his mother's reminder and got stuck in the rain.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) Where are you going?
Correct capitalization and question mark for a question.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Visiting grandparents is special. _______. For example, my grandmother teaches me how to cook traditional recipes."
Correct Answer: B) They share their wisdom and pass down traditions.
Connects to the example about grandmother teaching traditional recipes.

Reading Passage: "The Silent Hunter" (Q17-18)

I crouch low in the tall grass, my golden fur helping me blend in. My eyes are locked on the deer grazing nearby. I must be patient.

When the moment is right, I will run faster than any animal on land. My family is counting on me to bring back dinner. Being the leader of the pride is a big responsibility.

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A lion/lioness
Golden fur, hunts deer, leads a pride = lion.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator has golden fur, hunts deer, leads a pride, and runs fast
Classic lion characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about trees. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What is a tree's least favorite month? Sep-timber!
A pun on "September" and "timber" (cut trees).
Q20 Choose the best reply: Friend: "Do you want to come to my house to play?" You: ___
Correct Answer: B) Yes! That sounds like fun! What time should I come?
Enthusiastic and polite acceptance of invitation.
