Reading Passage: "The Rainbow After Rain"

Q1Why was Aisha sad at the beginning?
A) She didn't have any friends - Wrong: She had friends coming to her party
B) It was raining on her birthday - CORRECT: She had planned a garden party but couldn't play outside
C) She didn't get any presents - Wrong: Not mentioned in the story
D) Her grandmother was sick - Wrong: Grandmother was there to comfort her
Q2What "special gift" did the rain bring?
A) A cake - Wrong: Not mentioned
B) New toys - Wrong: Not mentioned
C) A rainbow - CORRECT: "A beautiful rainbow stretched across the sky!"
D) More rain - Wrong: The rain stopped
Q3How many colors of the rainbow are mentioned?
A) Four - Wrong: More colors are listed
B) Five - Wrong: More colors are listed
C) Six - CORRECT: "red, orange, yellow, green, blue, and purple"
D) Seven - Wrong: Only six colors are specifically mentioned
Q4How did Aisha feel at the end?
A) Still sad - Wrong: She said it was the best birthday
B) Very happy - CORRECT: "Aisha said it was the best birthday ever"
C) Angry - Wrong: No anger mentioned
D) Sleepy - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q5What lesson does grandmother teach?
A) Rain is always bad - Wrong: Opposite message
B) Never have birthday parties - Wrong: Not the lesson
C) Good things come to those who wait patiently - CORRECT: "the best surprises come after we wait patiently"
D) Rainbows are not real - Wrong: Opposite shown in story

Poem: "The Busy Bee"

Q6What does the bee collect?
A) Leaves - Wrong: Not mentioned
B) Nectar - CORRECT: "Collecting nectar all day long"
C) Seeds - Wrong: Not mentioned
D) Water - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q7Where does the bee go at end of day?
A) To another garden - Wrong: Goes home
B) To sleep under a leaf - Wrong: Goes to hive
C) Back to the hive - CORRECT: "Back to the hive before it's dark"
D) To the river - Wrong: Not mentioned
Q8Rhyme for "bright"
A) place - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme
B) sight - CORRECT: Rhymes with "bright"
C) day - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme
D) thing - Wrong: Doesn't rhyme

Language Skills

Q9Setting with snow, fireplace, warm soup
A) A summer beach scene - Wrong: Snow indicates winter
B) A warm cozy room in winter - CORRECT: Snow, fireplace, warm soup = winter indoors
C) A school classroom - Wrong: Fireplace not in schools
D) A busy marketplace - Wrong: Family is inside
Q10Persuade teacher for class outside
A) The classroom has desks and chairs - Wrong: Just a fact
B) Learning outside would be fun and we could study nature up close! - CORRECT: Gives benefits
C) Trees are green - Wrong: Just a fact
D) Some schools don't have playgrounds - Wrong: Not persuasive
Q11Order sentences: morning routine
A) 2, 3, 1, 4 - CORRECT: First brush teeth, then breakfast, shoes, finally school
B) 1, 2, 3, 4 - Wrong: Doesn't follow "First, Then, After, Finally"
C) 4, 3, 2, 1 - Wrong: Backwards order
D) 2, 1, 3, 4 - Wrong: Shoes before breakfast doesn't make sense
Q12Topic sentence for sandwich instructions
A) Sandwiches are healthy - Wrong: Doesn't introduce instructions
B) Here is how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich - CORRECT: Introduces the how-to steps
C) I like to eat lunch - Wrong: Too general
D) Bread is made from wheat - Wrong: Irrelevant
Q13"Raining cats and dogs" meaning
A) Cats and dogs falling from sky - Wrong: Literal interpretation
B) It was raining very heavily - CORRECT: This idiom means heavy rain
C) There were pets outside - Wrong: Not the meaning
D) Animals playing in rain - Wrong: Not the meaning
Q14Narrator's feeling about Grandma
A) Bored and tired - Wrong: Says "love visiting"
B) Happy and loving - CORRECT: "I love visiting her more than anything"
C) Scared and nervous - Wrong: No fear mentioned
D) Angry and sad - Wrong: Opposite feelings
Q15Correct punctuation for exclamation
A) what a beautiful day it is! - Wrong: No capital
B) What a beautiful day it is. - Wrong: Needs exclamation mark
C) What a beautiful day it is! - CORRECT: Capital + exclamation mark
D) what a Beautiful Day it is! - Wrong: Wrong capitalization
Q16Reason for "reading is great hobby"
A) Books have many pages - Wrong: Irrelevant
B) Libraries have thousands of books - Wrong: Irrelevant
C) Books help us learn new things - CORRECT: Matches dinosaur example
D) Some books are very expensive - Wrong: Irrelevant

Reading: "A Strange Morning"

Q17Who is telling the story?
A) A dog - Wrong: Dogs bark, don't meow
B) A cat - CORRECT: Four legs, paw, meows, kibble, windowsill nap
C) A child - Wrong: Children don't meow
D) A bird - Wrong: Birds don't have four legs
Q18Clues identifying narrator
A) Four legs, cleans with paw, meows - CORRECT: All cat behaviors
B) Goes to school - Wrong: Not mentioned
C) Flies - Wrong: Cats don't fly
D) Swims - Wrong: Cats don't typically swim
Q19Entertain with food joke
A) We need to eat food to stay healthy - Wrong: Information, not joke
B) What did the hungry computer eat? Microchips! - CORRECT: A pun/joke
C) Vegetables are good for you - Wrong: Information
D) Breakfast is the first meal - Wrong: Information
Q20Reply to party invitation
A) When is your birthday? - Wrong: She said it's a party
B) I would love to! Thank you for inviting me. - CORRECT: Polite acceptance
C) Parties are loud - Wrong: Rude
D) I already went to a party - Wrong: Irrelevant
