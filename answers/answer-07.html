Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Wishing Well" (Q1-5)

Q1 Correct Answer: B) People believed wishes made there came true

The story states "People said that if you threw a coin in and made a wish, it would come true."

Q2 Correct Answer: D) Mr. Khan's health to improve

Zoya whispered, "I wish for Mr. Khan to feel better" after seeing him struggling.

Q3 Correct Answer: B) She saw Mr. Khan struggling and wanted to help

Zoya saw Mr. Khan struggling with groceries and his bent back, so she chose to help him instead of wishing for toys.

Q4 Correct Answer: C) Mr. Khan's health improved

The village doctor offered free checkups and Mr. Khan's health improved.

Q5 Correct Answer: B) Kindness and thinking of others brings true happiness

Zoya "felt happier than any toy could make her" - showing that helping others brings greater happiness than material things.

Poem: "The Ocean" (Q6-8)

Q6 Correct Answer: B) Dolphins, whales, and fish

The poem mentions "Dolphins that dance and whales that sing, Fish of all colors."

Q7 Correct Answer: B) Sing

The poem says "whales that sing" - dolphins dance, while whales sing.

Q8 Correct Answer: C) hand

"Hand" rhymes with "sand" in "Seashells scattered along the sand."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Correct Answer: B) A campsite at night

Tent, trees, stars, campfire, and marshmallows all indicate camping at night.

Q10 Correct Answer: B) If I finish my homework and chores early, may I please stay up a little longer tonight?

This is persuasive because it offers responsible behavior in exchange for the request.

Q11 Correct Answer: A) 2, 3, 4, 1

Egg cycle: First (2) hen lays egg, Next (3) sits on it, Then (4) baby grows, Finally (1) chick breaks out.

Q12 Correct Answer: B) Our solar system is full of amazing planets.

This topic sentence introduces the paragraph about planets in our solar system.

Q13 Correct Answer: B) Mom told them firmly what she thought

"Give someone a piece of your mind" means to tell them clearly and firmly what you think, usually when upset.

Q14 Correct Answer: B) A sudden rainstorm during playtime

The passage describes children playing when rain suddenly started - a sudden rainstorm during playtime.

Q15 Correct Answer: B) Sara asked, "Can I have some water?"

Correct punctuation: capital letters, comma before quote, quotation marks, question mark inside quotes.

Q16 Correct Answer: B) It helps keep your body healthy.

This reason connects "walking is good for you" with the example about exercise and fresh air.

Reading Passage: "The Surprise Visitor" (Q17-18)

Q17 Correct Answer: B) A grandchild

The narrator calls the visitor "Grandma" and receives a hug - this is from a grandchild's perspective.

Q18 Correct Answer: C) Very happy and joyful

The narrator "shouted with joy" and ended with "an even fuller heart" - clearly very happy.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 Correct Answer: B) What's a vegetable's favorite martial art? Karate, because it's all about the "chop"!

This is a joke using wordplay - "chop" relates to both karate and chopping vegetables.

Q20 Correct Answer: C) I'm sorry to hear that. Do you want to talk about it?

This shows empathy and offers support - the appropriate response to a sad friend.
