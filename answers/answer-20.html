Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Giving Boy" (Q1-5)

Every week, Hamza saved some of his allowance money in a jar. His friends spent their money on toys and candy, but Hamza had a different plan.

At the end of the year, Hamza had saved enough to buy warm blankets. He and his parents took them to a shelter where people without homes could stay warm in winter.

A little boy at the shelter hugged his new blanket tightly. "Thank you," he whispered. Hamza felt his heart grow bigger.

"Why do you give your money away?" his friend asked later.

"Because making others happy makes me happier than any toy ever could," Hamza replied.

Q1 What did Hamza do with his allowance money?
Correct Answer: B) Saved it in a jar
"Every week, Hamza saved some of his allowance money in a jar."
Q2 What did Hamza buy with his saved money?
Correct Answer: C) Warm blankets
"Hamza had saved enough to buy warm blankets."
Q3 Where did Hamza take the blankets?
Correct Answer: B) To a shelter
"He and his parents took them to a shelter."
Q4 How did the little boy at the shelter react?
Correct Answer: B) He hugged the blanket and said thank you
"A little boy at the shelter hugged his new blanket tightly. 'Thank you,' he whispered."
Q5 What did Hamza learn about happiness?
Correct Answer: C) Helping others brings more happiness than buying things for yourself
"Making others happy makes me happier than any toy ever could."

Poem: "The Wind" (Q6-8)

The wind is a traveler that never rests,
Visiting mountains, valleys, and nests.
Sometimes it whispers, soft and light,
Sometimes it howls throughout the night.

It carries seeds to places far,
And pushes clouds past moon and star.
Though we cannot see its face,
The wind is felt in every place.

Q6 According to the poem, what does the wind do?
Correct Answer: B) Travels everywhere
"The wind is a traveler that never rests, Visiting mountains, valleys, and nests."
Q7 What does the wind carry according to the poem?
Correct Answer: B) Seeds and clouds
"It carries seeds to places far, And pushes clouds past moon and star."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "The wind can be gentle, the wind can be strong, It whistles a tune like a beautiful ___________."
Correct Answer: B) song
"Song" rhymes with "strong."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "The band played loud music on stage. People in the crowd clapped and danced. Colorful lights flashed in time with the music."
Correct Answer: A) A concert
Band, stage, crowd, dancing, colorful lights = concert.
Q10 You want to persuade your family to have pizza for dinner. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Let us order pizza tonight! Everyone loves it and we can each choose our favorite toppings!
Persuasive with enthusiasm and benefits.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Then, I blew out the candles. 2. First, everyone sang "Happy Birthday." 3. Finally, we ate the delicious cake. 4. Next, I made a wish.
Correct Answer: C) 2, 4, 1, 3
Birthday: sang (2), made wish (4), blew candles (1), ate cake (3).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. They help bees make honey. Flowers also make gardens and parks look beautiful. Some flowers even provide medicine for people."
Correct Answer: B) Flowers are important to people and nature.
Topic sentence matches content about bees, beauty, and medicine.
Q13 What does "the early bird catches the worm" mean? "Mom always says 'the early bird catches the worm' when she wakes me up for school."
Correct Answer: B) If you start early, you have an advantage
"The early bird catches the worm" = starting early gives you an advantage.
Q14 What helped the students pass the hard test? "Everyone thought the science test would be impossible. But when the results came, almost everyone passed! The secret was that the whole class had studied together and helped each other."
Correct Answer: B) They studied together and helped each other
"The secret was that the whole class had studied together and helped each other."
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: C) Wow! You did an amazing job!
Correct capitalization and exclamation marks.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Reading before bed is a good habit. _______. For example, I feel calm and sleepy after reading my favorite book."
Correct Answer: B) It helps you relax and fall asleep easier.
Connects to example of feeling calm and sleepy.

Reading Passage: "The Journey" (Q17-18)

I paddled through the cold stream, jumping over rocks and swimming through rapids. Soon I would reach the place where I was born. It has been a long journey from the ocean.

Many of my brothers and sisters did not make it this far. But I keep swimming upstream. I must lay my eggs in the same river where my life began. This is what we have done for thousands of years.

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A salmon/fish
Swims upstream, came from ocean, lays eggs = salmon.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator swims upstream from the ocean to lay eggs where it was born
Classic salmon migration behavior.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about food. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) What do you call cheese that is not yours? Nacho cheese!
A pun on "nacho" sounding like "not your."
Q20 Choose the best reply: Teacher: "Why were you late to class?" Student: ___
Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, my alarm did not go off. It will not happen again.
Polite apology with explanation and promise.
