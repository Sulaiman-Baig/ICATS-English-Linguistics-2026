Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Giving Boy" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) Saved it in a jar

"Every week, Hamza saved some of his allowance money in a jar."

Q2Correct Answer: C) Warm blankets

"Hamza had saved enough to buy warm blankets."

Q3Correct Answer: B) To a shelter

"He and his parents took them to a shelter."

Q4Correct Answer: B) He hugged the blanket and said thank you

"A little boy at the shelter hugged his new blanket tightly. 'Thank you,' he whispered."

Q5Correct Answer: C) Helping others brings more happiness than buying things for yourself

"Making others happy makes me happier than any toy ever could."

Poem: "The Wind" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) Travels everywhere

"The wind is a traveler that never rests, Visiting mountains, valleys, and nests."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Seeds and clouds

"It carries seeds to places far, And pushes clouds past moon and star."

Q8Correct Answer: B) song

"Song" rhymes with "strong."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: A) A concert

Band, stage, crowd, dancing, colorful lights = concert.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Let us order pizza tonight!...

Persuasive with enthusiasm and benefits.

Q11Correct Answer: C) 2, 4, 1, 3

Birthday: sang (2), made wish (4), blew candles (1), ate cake (3).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Flowers are important to people and nature.

Topic sentence matches content about bees, beauty, and medicine.

Q13Correct Answer: B) If you start early, you have an advantage

"The early bird catches the worm" = starting early gives you an advantage.

Q14Correct Answer: B) They studied together and helped each other

"The secret was that the whole class had studied together and helped each other."

Q15Correct Answer: C) Wow! You did an amazing job!

Correct capitalization and exclamation marks.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It helps you relax and fall asleep easier.

Connects to example of feeling calm and sleepy.

Reading Passage: "The Journey" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A salmon/fish

Swims upstream, came from ocean, lays eggs = salmon.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator swims upstream from the ocean to lay eggs where it was born

Classic salmon migration behavior.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) What do you call cheese that is not yours? Nacho cheese!

A pun on "nacho" sounding like "not your."

Q20Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, my alarm did not go off. It will not happen again.

Polite apology with explanation and promise.
