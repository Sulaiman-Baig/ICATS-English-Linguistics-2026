Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Balloon Adventure" (Q1-5)

Little Sana held tightly to her red balloon at the fair. It was the prettiest balloon she had ever seen. But then, a gust of wind blew it right out of her hand!

"My balloon!" Sana cried as she watched it float higher and higher into the sky.

Her father hugged her. "I know it is sad, but think about where your balloon might travel. Maybe it will float over mountains and oceans!"

Sana wiped her tears and smiled a little. "Maybe another child will find it and be happy."

That night, Sana dreamed of her red balloon having amazing adventures all around the world.

Q1 Where was Sana when she lost her balloon?
Correct Answer: C) At the fair
"Little Sana held tightly to her red balloon at the fair."
Q2 How did Sana lose her balloon?
Correct Answer: C) The wind blew it away
"A gust of wind blew it right out of her hand!"
Q3 How did Sana feel at first?
Correct Answer: C) Sad
She cried and needed comforting from her father.
Q4 What did her father say to make her feel better?
Correct Answer: B) The balloon might travel to amazing places
"Maybe it will float over mountains and oceans!"
Q5 What did Sana dream about?
Correct Answer: B) Her balloon having adventures around the world
"Sana dreamed of her red balloon having amazing adventures all around the world."

Poem: "The Rainbow" (Q6-8)

After the rain has gone away,
A rainbow comes out to play.
Red, orange, yellow, green, and blue,
With purple and violet painting the view.

An arch of colors in the sky,
A beautiful sight for you and I.
It does not last very long,
But its beauty makes us strong.

Q6 When does the rainbow appear?
Correct Answer: B) After the rain
"After the rain has gone away, A rainbow comes out to play."
Q7 How many colors are mentioned in the poem?
Correct Answer: C) Seven
Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, and violet = 7 colors.
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "The colors shine so very bright, Making everything feel just ___________."
Correct Answer: B) right
"Right" rhymes with "bright."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? "Rows and rows of books lined the shelves. A librarian stamped a book at the front desk. Children sat quietly at tables, reading and doing homework."
Correct Answer: B) A library
Books on shelves, librarian, quiet reading = library.
Q10 You want to persuade your parents to let you stay home from school. What should you write? (For a valid reason)
Correct Answer: B) I am not feeling well. I have a headache and feel dizzy. I think I need to rest today.
Valid reason with specific symptoms.
Q11 Put the sentences in order: 1. Next, we ate sandwiches and fruit. 2. First, we packed a basket with food. 3. Finally, we cleaned up and went home. 4. Then, we spread a blanket under a tree.
Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3
Picnic order: pack food (2), spread blanket (4), eat (1), clean up (3).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence: "_______. You need flour, sugar, eggs, and butter. First, mix all the dry ingredients. Then add the wet ingredients. Pour the batter into a pan and bake for 30 minutes."
Correct Answer: A) Here is how to make a simple cake.
Topic sentence about making cake matches the recipe instructions.
Q13 What does "feeling blue" mean? "Sara was feeling blue after her best friend moved away."
Correct Answer: B) Sara was feeling sad
"Feeling blue" = feeling sad.
Q14 What surprised people about the movie? "The movie was almost three hours long. Some people thought it would be boring, but the action and funny characters kept everyone entertained until the very end."
Correct Answer: B) It was entertaining despite being long
People expected it to be boring but it was entertaining.
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) Yesterday, I went to the mall.
Correct capitalization, comma after introductory word, period at end.
Q16 Which reason fits best? "Having good friends is important. _______. For example, when I was sick, my friends visited me and brought me homework."
Correct Answer: B) Good friends support you and help you when you need it.
Connects to example of friends visiting when sick.

Reading Passage: "Early Morning" (Q17-18)

The sun was just coming up. I stretched my long neck and reached for the tender leaves at the top of the acacia tree. Breakfast is best when the leaves are still wet with morning dew.

My baby stayed close to me, learning which plants are good to eat. She is getting taller every day! Soon she will be able to reach the high branches on her own.

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A giraffe
Long neck, eats leaves from tall trees, baby getting taller = giraffe.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator has a long neck and eats leaves from tall trees
These are distinctive giraffe characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about math. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Why was six afraid of seven? Because seven eight nine!
A pun on "eight" sounding like "ate."
Q20 Choose the best reply: Friend: "Would you like to come to my house after school?" You: ___
Correct Answer: B) That sounds fun! Let me ask my parents first.
Polite, enthusiastic, and responsible response.
