Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Balloon Adventure" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: C) At the fair

"Little Sana held tightly to her red balloon at the fair."

Q2Correct Answer: C) The wind blew it away

"A gust of wind blew it right out of her hand!"

Q3Correct Answer: C) Sad

She cried and needed comforting from her father.

Q4Correct Answer: B) The balloon might travel to amazing places

"Maybe it will float over mountains and oceans!"

Q5Correct Answer: B) Her balloon having adventures around the world

"Sana dreamed of her red balloon having amazing adventures all around the world."

Poem: "The Rainbow" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: B) After the rain

"After the rain has gone away, A rainbow comes out to play."

Q7Correct Answer: C) Seven

Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple, and violet = 7 colors.

Q8Correct Answer: B) right

"Right" rhymes with "bright."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A library

Books on shelves, librarian, quiet reading = library.

Q10Correct Answer: B) I am not feeling well...

Valid reason with specific symptoms.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3

Picnic order: pack food (2), spread blanket (4), eat (1), clean up (3).

Q12Correct Answer: A) Here is how to make a simple cake.

Topic sentence about making cake matches the recipe instructions.

Q13Correct Answer: B) Sara was feeling sad

"Feeling blue" = feeling sad.

Q14Correct Answer: B) It was entertaining despite being long

People expected it to be boring but it was entertaining.

Q15Correct Answer: B) Yesterday, I went to the mall.

Correct capitalization, comma after introductory word, period at end.

Q16Correct Answer: B) Good friends support you and help you when you need it.

Connects to example of friends visiting when sick.

Reading Passage: "Early Morning" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A giraffe

Long neck, eats leaves from tall trees, baby getting taller = giraffe.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator has a long neck and eats leaves from tall trees

These are distinctive giraffe characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Why was six afraid of seven? Because seven eight nine!

A pun on "eight" sounding like "ate."

Q20Correct Answer: B) That sounds fun! Let me ask my parents first.

Polite, enthusiastic, and responsible response.
