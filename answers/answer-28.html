Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Community Garden" (Q1-5)

Nobody used the empty lot on Maple Street until Mrs. Begum had an idea. "Let us turn it into a community garden!" she suggested.

At first, only a few neighbors helped. They pulled weeds, brought in soil, and planted seeds. Slowly, more people joined.

Now, the garden is full of vegetables, flowers, and fruit trees. Families come together to water, weed, and harvest. Children learn how plants grow. Older people share gardening tips.

"The best thing about this garden," Mrs. Begum says, "is not the food - it is how it brought our community together."

Q1 What was on Maple Street before the garden?
Correct Answer: B) An empty lot
"Nobody used the empty lot on Maple Street."
Q2 Who had the idea for the garden?
Correct Answer: C) Mrs. Begum
"Mrs. Begum had an idea. 'Let us turn it into a community garden!'"
Q3 How many people helped at first?
Correct Answer: B) Only a few neighbors
"At first, only a few neighbors helped."
Q4 What do children learn in the garden?
Correct Answer: B) How plants grow
"Children learn how plants grow."
Q5 What does Mrs. Begum say is the best thing about the garden?
Correct Answer: C) How it brought the community together
"The best thing... is how it brought our community together."

Poem: "Clouds" (Q6-8)

Clouds are fluffy, clouds are white,
Floating softly, what a sight!
Some look like animals in the sky,
Elephants and bunnies passing by.

When they turn gray, rain may fall,
Watering flowers, trees, and all.
Clouds are nature's floating art,
Each one special, set apart.

Q6 What color are most clouds?
Correct Answer: C) White
"Clouds are fluffy, clouds are white."
Q7 What happens when clouds turn gray?
Correct Answer: B) Rain may fall
"When they turn gray, rain may fall."
Q8 Complete the poem with a word that rhymes: "I lie on the grass and watch them go, Drifting gently, high and ___________."
Correct Answer: D) slow
"Slow" rhymes with "go."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9 Which picture best matches the setting? (Instruments, piano, music stands)
Correct Answer: B) A music room
Instruments, piano, music stands = music room.
Q10 You want to persuade your class to donate books to children who need them. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Let us share our books with children who do not have any. Our old books can bring them joy and help them learn!
Persuasive writing with appeal to help others.
Q11 Put the sentences in order (baking a cake story)
Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3
Baking: mixed (2), poured (4), baked (1), decorated (3).
Q12 Add the best topic sentence (paragraph about stars)
Correct Answer: B) Stars are fascinating objects in space.
Topic sentence about stars matches the paragraph.
Q13 What does "get cold feet" mean?
Correct Answer: B) She got nervous and did not want to do it
"Cold feet" = becoming nervous or scared to do something.
Q14 Why did Ayesha stay up late?
Correct Answer: B) She wanted to finish her exciting book
"She was on the last chapter of her mystery book!"
Q15 Choose the sentence with the correct punctuation.
Correct Answer: B) We went to Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.
Correct capitalization and commas in a list.
Q16 Which reason fits best with the opinion and example? (Keeping promises)
Correct Answer: B) It builds trust and shows people they can count on you.
Connects to the example about friend trusting her.

Reading Passage: "Down in the Burrow" (Q17-18)

It is cozy down here underground. My home has many tunnels and rooms. I store acorns and nuts for the winter when food is hard to find.

My cheeks can hold lots of food at once! I stuff them full and scurry back to my burrow. When it gets cold outside, I will be safe and warm with plenty to eat.

Q17 Who is telling this story?
Correct Answer: B) A squirrel or chipmunk
Stores acorns, cheeks hold food, lives in burrow = chipmunk/squirrel.
Q18 What clues tell you who the narrator is?
Correct Answer: A) The narrator stores acorns, has stretchy cheeks, and lives in a burrow
Classic chipmunk/squirrel characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19 You want to entertain someone with a joke about money. What should you write?
Correct Answer: B) Where does a penguin keep its money? In a snow bank!
A pun on "snow bank" (pile of snow and bank for money).
Q20 Choose the best reply: Teacher says "Please stop talking and pay attention."
Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, I will pay attention now.
Polite and respectful response to teacher.
