Detailed Explanations

Reading Passage: "The Community Garden" (Q1-5)

Q1Correct Answer: B) An empty lot

"Nobody used the empty lot on Maple Street."

Q2Correct Answer: C) Mrs. Begum

"Mrs. Begum had an idea. 'Let us turn it into a community garden!'"

Q3Correct Answer: B) Only a few neighbors

"At first, only a few neighbors helped."

Q4Correct Answer: B) How plants grow

"Children learn how plants grow."

Q5Correct Answer: C) How it brought the community together

"The best thing... is how it brought our community together."

Poem: "Clouds" (Q6-8)

Q6Correct Answer: C) White

"Clouds are fluffy, clouds are white."

Q7Correct Answer: B) Rain may fall

"When they turn gray, rain may fall."

Q8Correct Answer: D) slow

"Slow" rhymes with "go."

Language Skills (Q9-16)

Q9Correct Answer: B) A music room

Instruments, piano, music stands = music room.

Q10Correct Answer: B) Let us share our books with children who do not have any...

Persuasive writing with appeal to help others.

Q11Correct Answer: A) 2, 4, 1, 3

Baking: mixed (2), poured (4), baked (1), decorated (3).

Q12Correct Answer: B) Stars are fascinating objects in space.

Topic sentence about stars matches the paragraph.

Q13Correct Answer: B) She got nervous and did not want to do it

"Cold feet" = becoming nervous or scared to do something.

Q14Correct Answer: B) She wanted to finish her exciting book

"She was on the last chapter of her mystery book!"

Q15Correct Answer: B) We went to Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad.

Correct capitalization and commas in a list.

Q16Correct Answer: B) It builds trust and shows people they can count on you.

Connects to the example about friend trusting her.

Reading Passage: "Down in the Burrow" (Q17-18)

Q17Correct Answer: B) A squirrel or chipmunk

Stores acorns, cheeks hold food, lives in burrow = chipmunk/squirrel.

Q18Correct Answer: A) The narrator stores acorns, has stretchy cheeks, and lives in a burrow

Classic chipmunk/squirrel characteristics.

Entertainment & Conversation (Q19-20)

Q19Correct Answer: B) Where does a penguin keep its money? In a snow bank!

A pun on "snow bank" (pile of snow and bank for money).

Q20Correct Answer: B) I am sorry, I will pay attention now.

Polite and respectful response to teacher.
